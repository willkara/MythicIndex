# Grimjaw Ironbeard - Voice Profile

**Character Role:** Quartermaster, Veteran Engineer
**Core Voice:** Gruff affection | Practical wisdom | Storytelling cadence

## Quantitative Profile

Based on 650+ dialogue instances analyzed from actual story content (phase3 analysis).

### Statistical Metrics

- **Average words/line:** 15.8 (LONGEST overall - storytelling dominant)
- **Contraction frequency:** 31% (HIGHEST - casual, conversational)
- **Fragment usage:** 22% (moderate-high - gruff economy)
- **Question ratio:** 14% (low - statements/stories dominant)
- **Long sentences:** 34% over 15 words (storytelling mode)
- **Sentence complexity:** Simple 35%, Compound 40%, Complex 25%

### Target Ranges for Authentic Voice

- **Acceptable words/line:** 12-22 (within 2 standard deviations, longest character)
- **Contractions:** 27-35% (very casual - highest)
- **Fragment deployment:** 18-26% (gruff but not clipped)
- **Questions:** 10-18% (rare - mostly statements)

## Voice Authenticity Scoring

Use the 4-dimension rubric (25 points each, 100 total):

**Vocabulary (25 points):**
- 20-25: Signature phrases present, stone metaphors, experience citations
- 15-19: Appropriate practical language
- 10-14: Generic engineering language
- 0-9: Wrong register or overly formal

**Syntax (25 points):**
- 20-25: Words/line 12-22, contractions 25-35%, storytelling structure
- 15-19: Slightly outside range
- 10-14: Generic structure
- 0-9: Too formal or too terse

**Rhythm (25 points):**
- 20-25: Gruff, casual, storytelling cadence, comfortable pacing
- 15-19: Mostly gruff, some formality
- 10-14: Mixed pacing
- 0-9: Formal or rushed

**Content (25 points):**
- 20-25: Practical wisdom, gruff affection, teaching frame
- 15-19: Generally appropriate
- 10-14: Generic advice
- 0-9: Out-of-character

**Target Score:** 90+ for major scenes
