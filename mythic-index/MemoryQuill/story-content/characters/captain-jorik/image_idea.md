# Image Ideas for Captain Jorik

## Current Imagery
- **2 images:**
  1. Leading cavalry charge (action-shot, cinematic)
  2. Standing with horse (full-body portrait, somber)

## Character Summary
Grizzled veteran cavalry captain serving under Commander Atlock for decades. Leads the northern squadron in the Battle of Westwall. Professional, thorough, experienced. Commands respect from troops. Provides tactical assessments and recommends the Last Light Company for official recognition.

---

## Proposed Image Ideas

### 1. Tactical Briefing with Atlock
**Scene:** Jorik at a map table with Commander Atlock, pointing at positions on a battle map, both men in serious discussion.
- **Pose/Action:** Leaning over table, armored finger indicating enemy positions, face lit by lamp light from below
- **Setting:** Command tent or damaged Westwall interior, maps and markers spread out
- **Mood:** Strategic minds at work, veteran partnership, the planning before the storm
- **Tags:** two-character, tactical, map-table, leadership

### 2. Walking Among the Fallen
**Scene:** Jorik moving through the battlefield aftermath, taking stock of the dead and wounded, his face showing the weight of command.
- **Pose/Action:** Walking slowly, helmet removed and held at side, looking down at the cost
- **Setting:** Westwall courtyard at dawn after the battle, covered bodies, exhausted survivors
- **Mood:** Heavy responsibility, cost of victory, somber reflection
- **Tags:** aftermath, somber, battlefield, emotional

### 3. Human Exhaustion
**Scene:** A quiet moment as Jorik removes his helmet, wipes sweat-matted gray hair from his forehead, allowing his age and weariness to show.
- **Pose/Action:** Seated on supply crate or stone, helmet in one hand, other hand running through hair
- **Setting:** Corner of the Westwall, removed from the bustle, stolen private moment
- **Mood:** The man beneath the armor, decades of service weighing heavy, vulnerability
- **Tags:** intimate-portrait, exhaustion, quiet-moment, human

### 4. Rally Point
**Scene:** Jorik mid-battle, arm raised with sword pointing, shouting orders to his cavalry squadron as they regroup for another charge.
- **Pose/Action:** Mounted, twisted in saddle to address his men, sword extended, mouth open in command
- **Setting:** Battlefield chaos in background, dust and movement, squadron forming behind him
- **Mood:** Leadership under fire, the rallying cry, command presence
- **Tags:** action, mounted, commanding, battle-scene

### 5. Morning Assessment
**Scene:** Jorik standing in the damaged Westwall courtyard at dawn, making notes on a wax tablet as he catalogues the destruction.
- **Pose/Action:** Standing upright, stylus in hand, eyes scanning the damage, breath steaming in cold air
- **Setting:** First light illuminating battle damage, frost on the ground, repair crews beginning work
- **Mood:** The grim duty of accounting, professional thoroughness, cold morning reality
- **Tags:** assessment, dawn, professional, atmospheric

### 6. Decades of Service
**Scene:** Close portrait study of Jorik's weathered face, showing the map of lines and scars that tell his story of service.
- **Pose/Action:** Direct gaze at viewer, expression of calm authority, perhaps slight squint from years of sun
- **Setting:** Neutral background, strong directional lighting emphasizing texture
- **Mood:** The veteran's face, experience written in flesh, quiet authority
- **Tags:** close-portrait, character-study, weathered, authoritative

## Visual Consistency Notes
- Late 50s to early 60s, solid sturdy frame
- Full white-gray beard (closely trimmed but thick)
- Gray hair, often sweat-matted under helmet
- Weather-beaten, ruddy and tanned skin
- Deep crow's feet and forehead furrows
- Dark, weary but focused eyes
- Battle-worn plate armor with scratches and dents
- Simple open-faced steel helmet with slight brim
- Plain cruciform-hilt arming sword
- Brown leather details, heavy riding boots
