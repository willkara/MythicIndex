# Mistress Elba - Profile

## Basic Information
- **Full Name**: Elba Ironspoon
- **Aliases/Nicknames**: Mistress Elba
- **Age**: 173 (grandmother age for a dwarf)
- **Gender**: Female
- **Role in Story**: Cook, Unofficial Morale Officer, Defender, Company Grandmother
- **First Appearance**: Chapter 8 - The Westwall Welcome
- **Status**: Active

## Physical Description
- **Height/Build**: 4'2", broad-shouldered and sturdy even by dwarven standards
- **Hair**: Iron-gray with copper streaks, braided in traditional style
- **Eyes**: Deep amber, sharp as a master chef's knife
- **Distinguishing Features**: Flour dusting her apron, faint burn scars on forearms from decades of cooking
- **Typical Clothing**: Practical dress and a well-worn apron.
- **Body Language**: No-nonsense, hands often on hips.
- **Physical Condition**: Strong from a lifetime of physical work.

## Visual Summary
Mistress Elba is a dwarven grandmother with a sturdy, broad-shouldered build. Her iron-gray hair, streaked with copper, is kept in traditional braids. She has sharp, deep amber eyes and a no-nonsense expression. She is almost always seen in a practical dress and a well-worn, flour-dusted apron, with faint burn scars on her forearms.

## Personality
- **Archetype**: The Team Mom / The Berserker Chef
- **Temperament**: No-nonsense, gruffly maternal, fiercely protective.
- **Positive Traits**: Caring, practical, brave, organized.
- **Negative Traits**: Can be overbearing.
- **Moral Alignment**: Lawful Good

## Skills & Abilities
- **Expertise**: Cooking for large groups, logistics, morale boosting, surprisingly effective with a cleaver.
- **Languages**: Common, Dwarvish
- **Education Level**: Traditional dwarven clan training, decades of practical cooking experience
- **Special Abilities**: Can make a nourishing meal out of anything. Stubborn as mountain stone about proper eating. Can sense when someone has skipped meals. Grandmother instincts that manifest as fierce protective caring. Knows exactly what everyone needs before they do. Darkvision (60 feet).

## Personal Details
- **Habits**: Ensuring everyone is fed, whether they think they're hungry or not.
- **Hobbies**: Cooking, organizing.
- **Personal Quarters**: A small room off the main kitchen.
- **Likes**: Well-stocked pantries, people enjoying her food, seeing "her boys and girls" safe.
- **Dislikes**: Waste, soldiers not eating enough, anyone threatening her kitchen or her people.
- **Fears**: People not eating properly. Her 'children' (Company members) getting hurt. Food going to waste. Anyone trying to cook in her kitchen without permission. Not being able to protect her found family.

## Notes
* Runs the mess at the Westwall Watchpost - took the job years ago for good pay and purpose.
* Acts as a stubborn maternal figure to the entire company, especially the younger members.
* During the Battle for Westwall, she defended the medical station by killing a mercenary with a kitchen cleaver, proving her fierce loyalty and courage.
* Played a key role in organizing the civilian relief effort after the battle, working with Mrs. Blackwood to feed hundreds.
* Has three adult children and seven grandchildren in Waterdeep's dwarven quarter whom she visits regularly. Happy, normal life outside work.
* Treats the Company like unruly children who need constant feeding and scolding, drawing on decades of grandmother experience.
* Often compares Company members to her actual grandchildren when mothering them.
