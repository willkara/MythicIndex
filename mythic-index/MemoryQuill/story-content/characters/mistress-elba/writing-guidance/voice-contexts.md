# Mistress Elba - Voice by Context

---

## Context Distribution

- **Maternal Command:** 60% (caring authority)
- **Feeding/Care:** 30% (food-focused)
- **Rare Concern:** 10% (worry for safety)

---

## Maternal Command Context (60%)

**Words/line:** 11.8 avg
**Key Markers:** "My boys/girls," commanding, affectionate authority

### Example

"My boys, you're not leaving without checking supplies. Eat something first. Non-negotiable."

---

## Feeding/Care Context (30%)

**Words/line:** 11.2 avg
**Key Markers:** Food, caring action, maternal focus

### Example

"Here. Fresh bread, stew, and honey. Eat. You'll need strength for what's ahead."

---

## Rare Concern Context (10%)

**Words/line:** 12.4 avg
**Key Markers:** "My," protective, underlying fear masked by authority

### Example

"My girls, you be careful out there. Come back to me whole. Understand?"

---

## Quick Guide

| Situation | Words/Line | Key Markers |
|-----------|-----------|---|
| **Command** | 11.8 | "My boys/girls," maternal authority, food often included |
| **Feeding** | 11.2 | Food action, caring instruction, nourishment focus |
| **Concern** | 12.4 | "My," protective, underlying worry, safety emphasis |
