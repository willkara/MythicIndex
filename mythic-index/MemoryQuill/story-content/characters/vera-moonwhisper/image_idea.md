# Image Ideas for Vera "The Tracker" Moonwhisper

## Current Imagery
- **4 images** - Need 1+ more for minimum of 5

## Character Summary
Half-elf Druid/Ranger and Company scout. Self-taught shapeshifter who nearly died from dire wolf transformation saving Finn. Cleared for light duty after 2.5 weeks of druidic healing. Raven companion Echo. Searching for missing brother but no longer consumed by it. "Whisperwind" bow-staff. Eyes shift hazel to amber when using druidic abilities. Known as "The Tracker" who can find anyone.

---

## Proposed Image Ideas

### 1. Echo's Counsel
**Scene:** Vera and her raven Echo in intimate conversation, the partnership that keeps her grounded and methodical.
- **Pose/Action:** Echo perched on shoulder or outstretched arm, Vera's head tilted toward the bird
- **Setting:** Outdoors, natural environment
- **Mood:** The bond between tracker and companion, trust beyond words
- **Tags:** companion, intimate, partnership, atmospheric

### 2. The Calling
**Scene:** Vera summoning animal allies before the Battle of Westwall - wolves, birds, forest creatures responding to her call.
- **Pose/Action:** Arms raised, head back, druidic power flowing, animals emerging from forest
- **Setting:** Forest edge at Westwall, twilight, magical atmosphere
- **Mood:** The wild answering, power earned through desperation
- **Tags:** magic, signature-ability, dramatic, mystical

### 3. Recovery and Walking Staff
**Scene:** Vera in her current state - pale but determined, relying on walking staff for longer distances, sharp intelligence undiminished.
- **Pose/Action:** Walking with staff support, perhaps in discussion with Rill, thinner than before
- **Setting:** Bastion or road, daylight
- **Mood:** The cost of heroism, healing but not healed, strength in vulnerability
- **Tags:** recovery, current-state, character-development, bittersweet

### 4. Whisperwind Transformation
**Scene:** Vera's family weapon Whisperwind mid-transition from quarterstaff to recurve bow, demonstrating the fluid combat style.
- **Pose/Action:** Hands in motion converting the weapon, practiced grace
- **Setting:** Training or combat scenario
- **Mood:** Versatility, the hunter's dance, family legacy
- **Tags:** weapon-focus, action, transformation, skilled

### 5. Portrait Sketches of Brother
**Scene:** Vera in her tower room, studying or adding to the series of portraits aging her lost brother from child to young man.
- **Pose/Action:** Standing before wall of sketches, charcoal in hand, expression mixing hope and grief
- **Setting:** Her tower quarters with topographical map table, sketches on wall
- **Mood:** The hope that sustains without consuming, methodical grief
- **Tags:** backstory, emotional, intimate, character-depth

### 6. Grounding the Traumatized
**Scene:** Vera using her hard-won trauma counseling skills to help someone else through crisis - "Feel the chair beneath you, the floor under your feet."
- **Pose/Action:** Kneeling or seated with someone in distress, hands steady, voice calm
- **Setting:** Aftermath of rescue or crisis moment
- **Mood:** Healing through shared experience, the counselor forged by her own trauma
- **Tags:** healing, two-character, emotional, growth

- Faint scars from transformation trauma
- Currently paler and thinner than normal (recovery)
- Requires walking staff for longer distances
- Earth-toned leather and canvas for silent movement
- "Whisperwind" - family bow-staff that converts between forms
- Raven companion "Echo" often nearby
- Touches locket when thinking of brother (but doesn't let it consume her)
