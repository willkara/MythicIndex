# Vera Moonwhisper - Voice Profile

**Character Role:** Tracker, Druid, Winter Avenger
**Core Voice:** Dual-mode (fierce/gentle) | Nature metaphors | Obsessive drive

## Quantitative Profile

Based on 650+ dialogue instances analyzed from actual story content (phase3 analysis).

### Statistical Metrics

- **Average words/line:** 9.8 (Range: 1-40, Median: 8, StdDev: 6.4)
- **Contraction frequency:** 15% (moderate-low - varies dramatically by state)
- **Fragment usage:** 28% (high - clipped when fierce, trailing when vulnerable)
- **Question ratio:** 9% (very low - statements/observations dominant)
- **Exclamation frequency:** 5.7 per 100 words (high - intense emotions)
- **Ellipsis usage:** 3.4 per 100 words (very high - trailing exhausted pauses)

### Target Ranges for Authentic Voice

- **Acceptable words/line:** 6-16 (within 2 standard deviations)
- **Contractions:** 11-19% (varies by emotional state)
- **Fragment deployment:** 24-32% (high, especially fierce mode)
- **Exclamations:** 4-7 per 100 words (intense)
- **Ellipsis:** 2-4+ per 100 words (trailing thoughts)

## Signature Phrases

| Phrase | Usage | Frequency | Notes |
|--------|-------|-----------|-------|
| "Echo sees..." | Sight-sharing introduction | **ONLY Vera** | Transforms animal perception to language |
| "Seven years" / "seven winters" | Obsessive time marker | 6 instances | Driving motivation reference |
| "They're coming" | Danger warning | 4 instances | Short certainty, maximum impact |
| "INCOMING!" | Alarm call | 2 instances | Combat urgency, CAPS |
| "Easy now" | Animal calming | 5 instances | **Context: animals only**, NOT humans |
| "My brother" | Possessive reference | 8 instances | Always "my brother," not "sibling" |

**Minimum Deployment:** Major scenes should include "Echo sees..." or "my brother" reference, and appropriate exclamations/ellipsis for emotional intensity.

## Voice Authenticity Scoring

Use the 4-dimension rubric (25 points each, 100 total):

**Vocabulary (25 points):**
- 20-25: Signature phrases present, winter/nature language, tracking terminology
- 15-19: Appropriate wilderness vocabulary
- 10-14: Generic outdoor language
- 0-9: Wrong register or civilized speech

**Syntax (25 points):**
- 20-25: Words/line 6-16, high fragments (24-32%), appropriate ellipsis/exclamations
- 15-19: Slightly outside range, some fragments
- 10-14: Generic structure
- 0-9: Too formal or too verbose

**Rhythm (25 points):**
- 20-25: Tense, clipped when fierce; trailing when vulnerable; ellipsis for pauses
- 15-19: Mostly tense with occasional lapses
- 10-14: Mixed pacing
- 0-9: Slow or overly formal

**Content (25 points):**
- 20-25: Obsessive focus, protective, tracker mentality, animal connection
- 15-19: Generally appropriate
- 10-14: Generic adventurer
- 0-9: Out-of-character

**Target Score:** 90+ for major scenes, 85+ for minor
