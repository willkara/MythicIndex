# Vera Moonwhisper - Dialogue Examples

**Quick Reference:** Vera is dual-mode (fierce/gentle), short-winded (9.8 words/line), uses high exclamations (5.7/100 words) and ellipsis (3.4/100 words). Signature "Echo sees..." and "my brother" references. Combat = caps + fragments. Tracking = precise detail. Vulnerable = ellipsis trailing off.

---

## Perfect Authenticity (95-100)

### Example 1: Combat Alarm
**Dialogue:** "INCOMING! EASTERN APPROACH! ALL POSITIONS!"

**Score:** 98/100 - All caps urgency, directional precision (3 exclamation points, fragments), combat fierce mode perfect

### Example 2: Echo's Perception
**Dialogue:** "Echo sees... old foundation, walls of stone half-buried in drifts, collapsed roof beams crossed like broken bones. Blue mitten caught on rusty iron. North-northeast, maybe three hundred yards."

**Score:** 97/100 - "Echo sees" frame, ellipsis for trance, precise distance/direction, vivid sensory detail

### Example 3: Danger Warning
**Dialogue:** "They're coming."

**Score:** 98/100 - Two words maximum impact, signature warning phrase, absolute certainty

## Acceptable (85-89)

### Example 6: Animal Comfort
**Dialogue:** "It's okay. Friends, not foes. Easy now."

**Score:** 87/100 - Gentle mode, animal context appropriate, 6 words

## Voice Violations (Below 75)

### Example 7: Over-Wordy Explanation
**Dialogue:** "Perhaps we should consider whether it might be advantageous to methodically examine the northern approach given the potential tactical implications."

**Why Wrong:** 22 words, formal language, zero personality, no exclamations, wrong Vera entirely

**Correct:** "North side. Check it. Now."

## Usage Guidelines

**When to Load:** Vera dialogue scenes, tracker/druid moments, combat situations, Echo references

**Scoring Your Vera:** Check words/line (6-16), verify exclamations (4-7/100), ensure ellipsis for exhausted moments, confirm "Echo sees" or "my brother" when appropriate

**Quick Test:** Is this clipped, intense, possibly trailing off? Is it tracker-precise or fiercely protective? If yes, likely authentic Vera.
