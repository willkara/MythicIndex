## Proposed Image Ideas

**Scene:** Standard military portrait of Sergeant Kass in clean, regulation City Watch uniform, the model of professional bearing.
- **Pose/Action:** Standing at attention, sharp salute, crisp posture
- **Setting:** Neutral military backdrop
- **Mood:** Professionalism, reliability, "by the book"
- **Tags:** primary-portrait, military, formal, clean

### 2. Reinforcements Arrive
**Scene:** Kass at the head of his ten-soldier column riding through the Westwall gates, bringing hope to the besieged defenders.

- **Mood:** Relief, cavalry arriving, hope renewed

### 3. Defensive Integration
**Scene:** Kass coordinating with Company members on the wall, pointing out positions, professional collaboration in action.

- **Pose/Action:** Shield up, spear braced, shouting encouragement to his men
- **Setting:** Battle at the breach, chaos around ordered formation
- **Mood:** Discipline holds when courage wavers, training saves lives

### 5. Aftermath Coordination
