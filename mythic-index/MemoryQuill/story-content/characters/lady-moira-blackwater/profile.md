# Lady Moira Blackwater - Profile

## Basic Information
- **Full Name**: Lady Moira Blackwater
- **Aliases/Nicknames**: The Spymaster (internal use), Lady Blackwater (formal)
- **Race**: Half-elf
- **Class**: Intelligence Official/Spymaster
- **Role in Story**: Lords' Intelligence Coordination Office Director, Company handler
- **First Appearance**: Chapter 21, Scene 1 (Silent Hand Revelation briefing)
- **Status**: Active

## Physical Description
- **Age**: Approximately 50 years (appears early 40s due to half-elf heritage)
- **Height/Build**: Average height, composed bearing that commands attention
- **Hair**: Silver-streaked auburn hair kept in a precise professional style
- **Eyes**: Sharp gray-green eyes that miss nothing, perpetually assessing
- **Distinguishing Features**: 
  - Wears understated but expensive clothing that speaks of authority without ostentation
  - Always carries a leather portfolio with official seals
  - Has small scars on her hands from early fieldwork
- **Typical Clothing**: Professional attire suitable for noble courts - well-tailored but practical
- **Physical Condition**: Excellent health, moves with the controlled grace of someone trained in combat but focused on mental warfare

## Visual Summary
Lady Moira Blackwater is a half-elf spymaster who appears to be in her early 40s. She has a composed, authoritative bearing and sharp, gray-green eyes that miss nothing. Her silver-streaked auburn hair is kept in a precise, professional style. She wears understated but expensive and practical clothing suitable for a high-ranking official.

## Personality
- **Archetype**: The Pragmatic Spymaster ("M" from James Bond)
- **Temperament**: Coolly professional, strategically minded, unflappably calm under pressure
- **Positive Traits**: Patriotic, exceptionally competent, respectful of ability, strategic thinker, decisive leader
- **Negative Traits**: Ruthlessly practical, emotionally distant, manipulative when necessary, comfortable with morally gray methods
- **Moral Alignment**: Lawful Neutral - serves Waterdeep's interests above all else, willing to authorize questionable methods for greater good
- **Decision-Making Style**: Assesses assets and situations with clinical detachment, grants operational flexibility when results matter more than methods

## Skills & Abilities
- **Expertise**: 
  - Intelligence analysis and strategic planning
  - Reading people and assessing capabilities
  - Government bureaucracy and noble court politics
  - Unconventional threat assessment
  - Asset management and handler operations
- **Special Abilities**: 
  - Decades of experience in intelligence work
  - Extensive network of contacts throughout Waterdeep and beyond
  - Access to resources and information unavailable to normal citizens
- **Languages**: Common, Elvish, Halfling, Draconic, various regional dialects
- **Combat Style**: Prefers to avoid combat but trained in defensive techniques and carries concealed weapons

## Personal Details
- **Background**: Career intelligence officer who rose through merit rather than birth
- **Philosophy**: "The city's security requires tools that operate beyond official constraints"
- **Motivations**: Protecting Waterdeep from threats that conventional forces cannot handle
- **Fears**: Threats that slip through official channels, loss of valuable assets
- **Habits**: Always carries multiple backup plans, studies people's reactions constantly
- **Hobbies**: Chess, strategic war games, reading intelligence reports from other cities

## Professional Role
- **Position**: Director, Lords' Intelligence Coordination Office
- **Responsibilities**: 
  - Managing unconventional threats to city security
  - Coordinating between various intelligence agencies
  - Deploying specialized assets for sensitive missions
  - Briefing the Lords' Committee on security matters
- **Authority Level**: Can authorize missions, allocate resources, provide official documentation
- **Reporting Structure**: Reports directly to select members of the Lords' Committee

## Relationship with Last Light Company
- **Professional Assessment**: Perfect deniable asset for missions requiring "flexibility" - particularly values their unconventional methods and proven tactical innovation
- **Strategic Value**: Skilled, morally motivated, independent operators with capabilities that exceed conventional forces
- **Management Approach**: Provides clear objectives, necessary resources, and operational freedom while carefully orchestrating their deployment for maximum effect
- **Long-term Vision**: Cultivate them as a reliable tool for problems beyond official capabilities, especially those requiring methods outside normal legal constraints
- **Personal Opinion**: Respects their competence while maintaining professional distance, fully aware of their individual capabilities (particularly Marcus's psychological warfare skills)
- **Operational Integration**: Coordinates their missions with existing intelligence operations, using their provisional status to maintain deniability

## Voice & Dialogue Patterns
- **Speech Style**: Precise, professional, uses intelligence terminology naturally with cultured precision
- **Signature Phrases**: 
  - "Your reputation for... creative solutions precedes you"
  - "Assets that prove reliable for critical missions tend to find their administrative processes... streamlined"
  - "Sometimes the city's needs require... flexibility"
  - "Remarkable tactical improvisation under impossible odds"
  - "Resources and methods that aren't constrained by official procedures"
- **Diplomatic Language**: Masters euphemism and implication while being perfectly clear about expectations - particularly skillful at veiled threats wrapped in bureaucratic language
- **Professional Tone**: Treats the Company as valued professionals rather than subordinates while maintaining clear authority hierarchy

## Background & History
- **Early Career**: Field operative who demonstrated exceptional analytical and strategic abilities
- **Rise to Authority**: Promoted through intelligence ranks based on successful handling of complex threats
- **Key Achievements**: 
  - Identified and neutralized several major threats to Waterdeep before they materialized
  - Developed protocols for managing unconventional security challenges
  - Built extensive intelligence network throughout the Sword Coast region
- **Current Focus**: Managing threats that require assets operating outside normal legal constraints

## Story Function
- **Mission Provider**: Delivers assignments that align with Company values but serve city interests
- **Political Buffer**: Provides official authorization and protection for Company operations
- **Intelligence Source**: Access to information and resources beyond Company's normal reach
- **Character Development Catalyst**: Forces Company to navigate government relationships while maintaining independence
- **Recurring Authority Figure**: "M" figure who respects their abilities while using them strategically

## Notes
- Represents the "benevolent handler" archetype - uses the Company but for legitimate government purposes
- Creates tension between Company independence and official obligations
- Provides pathway for larger, more significant missions while maintaining character agency
- Never personal antagonist - professional relationship based on mutual respect and utility
- Half-elf longevity means she has institutional memory and long-term strategic perspective
