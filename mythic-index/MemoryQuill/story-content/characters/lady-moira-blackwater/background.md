# Lady Moira Blackwater - Background

## Origin
- **Birthplace**: Waterdeep, Castle Ward
- **Birth Date**: Approximately 50 years ago
- **Social Class**: Professional class - earned position through merit rather than birth
- **Cultural Background**: Half-elf raised in Waterdeep's cosmopolitan noble district, understanding both human urgency and elvish patience

## Family
- **Parents**: Human mother (minor noble house), Elf father (diplomatic attaché)
- **Family Dynamics**: Parents encouraged service to the city; instilled both ambition and duty
- **Current Family Status**: Maintains professional distance from family to protect operational security

## History
- **Early Career (Ages 20-30)**: 
  - Started as junior analyst in City Watch Intelligence
  - Demonstrated exceptional ability to see patterns and assess threats
  - Volunteered for dangerous field assignments to prove capabilities
  
- **Field Operations (Ages 30-40)**:
  - Served as undercover operative in various Sword Coast cities
  - Specialized in identifying threats before they reached Waterdeep
  - Developed extensive network of contacts and sources
  
- **Rise to Leadership (Ages 40-50)**:
  - Promoted to Lords' Intelligence Coordination Office
  - Proved ability to manage complex, multi-agency operations
  - Became go-to handler for "unconventional assets" and sensitive missions

## Professional Career
- **Lords' Intelligence Coordination Office**: Elite intelligence agency serving Waterdeep's ruling council
- **Specialization**: Threats that don't fit normal categories - magical, criminal, or political
- **Management Philosophy**: Deploy the right tool for each specific problem
- **Success Record**: Consistently prevents major threats through strategic use of specialized assets

## Key Professional Relationships
- **Lords' Committee**: Reports to select members on security matters
- **City Watch**: Coordinates with but operates above their authority level
- **Unconventional Assets**: Maintains professional relationships with various specialists
- **International Contacts**: Network throughout Sword Coast for intelligence gathering

## Personal Philosophy
- **Core Belief**: Waterdeep's security requires tools that operate beyond official constraints
- **Professional Ethics**: Will use any legal method to protect the city
- **Asset Management**: Treat valuable assets with respect while using them effectively
- **Long-term Thinking**: Plan for threats that may not materialize for years

## How She Got Here
- **Path**: Field operative → Senior analyst → Asset manager → Department director
- **Key Insight**: Some threats require solutions that official channels cannot provide
- **Current Role**: Director of an office that doesn't officially exist, managing assets that officially aren't there
- **Future Goals**: Build sustainable network of specialists who can handle any threat to Waterdeep

## Personal Details
- **Living Situation**: Modest but secure residence near government district
- **Security Measures**: Extensive personal protection without being obvious about it
- **Work-Life Balance**: Work IS life - sees intelligence work as essential civic duty
- **Recreation**: Strategic games, reading intelligence from other cities, maintaining professional relationships
- **Favorite Meal**: Herb-crusted sea bass over lemoned farro. She prepares it herself on the rare evenings she is home, appreciating the precise timing required for perfect skin.
- **Favorite Drink**: A cut-crystal glass of dry elven vermouth with three drops of bitterroot tincture. She sips it while reviewing dossiers, savoring the clarity it promises more than the taste itself.
