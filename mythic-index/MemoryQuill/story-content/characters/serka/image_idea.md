# Image Ideas for Serka

## Current Imagery
- **2 images:**
  1. Portrait with twin axes crossed (aggressive, weapon-focus)
  2. Close-up snarling portrait in armor (menacing)

## Character Summary
Elite female orc fighter and member of Krag's Black Hawks mercenary company. Vicious, cruel, and sadistic. Attempted to execute the wounded Finn during the Battle of Westwall. Killed by Vera Moonwhisper's dire wolf transformation - the first victim of Vera's unleashed fury. **Status: Deceased.**

---

## Proposed Image Ideas

### 1. Standing Over Finn
**Scene:** The pivotal moment - Serka standing over the fallen, wounded Finn, twin axes raised for the killing blow, murder reflected in the polished steel.
- **Pose/Action:** Axes raised high overhead, feet planted wide, looking down at prey with savage satisfaction
- **Setting:** Westwall courtyard during battle, smoke and chaos around, Finn visible at her feet
- **Mood:** Cruelty triumphant (for a moment), predator with cornered prey, imminent death
- **Tags:** pivotal-moment, threatening, action-pose, dramatic

### 2. The Last Sight
**Scene:** Serka's face in the moment she realizes death is coming - terror replacing cruelty as massive wolf jaws fill her vision.
- **Pose/Action:** Eyes wide, mouth open in beginning of scream, axes frozen mid-swing
- **Setting:** Blur of silver-gray fur and teeth dominating the frame, chaos around
- **Mood:** Horror, tables turned, the predator become prey
- **Tags:** dramatic, horror, final-moment, intense

### 3. Battle Fury
**Scene:** Serka in full combat, twin axes swinging in deadly arcs, cutting through defenders with brutal efficiency.
- **Pose/Action:** Mid-swing, body twisted, tusks bared in battle-cry, muscles straining
- **Setting:** Breach in Westwall, other combatants around, dust and blood in air
- **Mood:** Violence personified, the brute in her element, savage joy
- **Tags:** action, combat, dynamic, brutal

### 4. Black Hawks Elite
**Scene:** Serka among Krag's elite guard, standing in formation with other dangerous mercenaries, projecting intimidating unity.
- **Pose/Action:** Standing with axes at rest but ready, chin raised, scarred face proud
- **Setting:** Mercenary camp or pre-battle assembly, other Black Hawks visible
- **Mood:** Elite status, professional killers, belonging among the worst
- **Tags:** group-scene, mercenary, intimidating, professional

### 5. Predator's Approach
**Scene:** Serka stalking through battlefield smoke, hunting for vulnerable targets, axes held low and ready.
- **Pose/Action:** Low stance, moving forward, head turning to track movement, predatory focus
- **Setting:** Smoke-filled battlefield, fires burning, visibility limited
- **Mood:** Hunter in her element, patient cruelty, seeking prey
- **Tags:** stalking, atmospheric, threatening, smoke

### 6. The Executioner's Smile
**Scene:** Close portrait of Serka's face showing her characteristic cruelty - the smile before the kill, tusks prominent, amber eyes holding "murder."
- **Pose/Action:** Slight smile curving around tusks, head tilted, predatory anticipation
- **Setting:** Dark background, sharp lighting emphasizing features
- **Mood:** Pure menace, the face of a killer enjoying their work
- **Tags:** close-portrait, villainous, menacing, character-study
