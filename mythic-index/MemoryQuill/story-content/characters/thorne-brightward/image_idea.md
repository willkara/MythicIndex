# Image Ideas for Captain Thorne Brightward

## Current Imagery
- **4 images** - Need 1+ more for minimum of 5

## Character Summary
Human paladin in early 40s serving as Deputy Commander of the Last Light Company. Steel-gray hair, deep-set brown eyes, weathered but kind face. Former City Guard captain who left on excellent terms. Defensive combat specialist known for "Brightward's Wall" shield formation. His horn signal is recognized as the arrival of the Last Light Company. Lawful Good but learning to "read between the lines."

---

## Proposed Image Ideas

### 1. The Horn Signal
**Scene:** Thorne on horseback, blowing his signature horn that can be heard for miles - the signal that help has arrived.
- **Pose/Action:** Mounted, horn raised to lips, cloak streaming behind
- **Setting:** Hill overlooking a crisis scene, dawn or dramatic lighting
- **Mood:** Hope arriving, the promise of rescue made audible
- **Tags:** signature-moment, iconic, mounted, dramatic

### 2. Brightward's Wall
**Scene:** Thorne executing his signature defensive formation, steel tower shield protecting multiple allies during combat.
- **Pose/Action:** Shield raised creating cover, sword ready, protecting teammates behind him
- **Setting:** Battle scene, allies visible behind his protective stance
- **Mood:** "Formation and discipline" - the rock everyone shelters behind
- **Tags:** combat, signature-move, protective, action

### 3. After-Action Report
**Scene:** Thorne at his desk writing meticulous after-action reports in neat, precise script, the administrative side of leadership.
- **Pose/Action:** Seated, quill in hand, journals and papers organized precisely
- **Setting:** His perfectly rectangular quarters, morning light through window
- **Mood:** Discipline in all things, the paperwork that keeps people alive
- **Tags:** intimate, administrative, character-depth, morning

### 4. Former Commission
**Scene:** Thorne standing before his framed City Guard commission on the wall, reflecting on a different kind of service.
- **Pose/Action:** Standing in contemplation, back to viewer, looking at the frame
- **Setting:** His quarters, the commission visible on the wall
- **Mood:** The past that shaped the present, duty transformed not abandoned
- **Tags:** reflective, backstory, emotional, quiet

### 5. Reading Between the Lines
**Scene:** The moment Thorne decides to bend his rigid principles for greater justice - perhaps the Stonebridge bluff or similar moral choice.
- **Pose/Action:** Expression showing internal conflict resolving into determination
- **Setting:** Tense situation requiring compromise
- **Mood:** Growth, the paladin learning flexibility, rules serving justice
- **Tags:** character-growth, moral-moment, determined, pivotal

### 6. Coordinating the Defense
**Scene:** Thorne in tactical mode, directing team movements with calm authority cutting through chaos.
- **Pose/Action:** Hand signals, verbal commands, positioning teammates with practiced precision
- **Setting:** Pre-battle or active combat situation
- **Mood:** "Formation Delta. Execute on my mark." - the coordinator in his element
- **Tags:** tactical, leadership, command, action
