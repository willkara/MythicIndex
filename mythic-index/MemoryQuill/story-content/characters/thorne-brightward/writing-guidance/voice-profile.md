# Thorne Brightward - Voice Profile

**Character Role:** Second-in-Command, Tactical Leader
**Core Voice:** Military precision | Protective | Formal with warmth beneath

## Quantitative Profile

Based on 650+ dialogue instances analyzed from actual story content (phase3 analysis).

### Statistical Metrics

- **Average words/line:** 11.4 (Range: 2-32, Median: 10, StdDev: 6.8)
- **Contraction frequency:** 22% (moderate-high, relaxed professionalism)
- **Fragment usage:** 12% (low - complete sentences preferred)
- **Question ratio:** 18% (moderate - tactical queries)
- **Sentence complexity:** Simple 42%, Compound 38%, Complex 20%

### Target Ranges for Authentic Voice

- **Acceptable words/line:** 8-16 (within 2 standard deviations)
- **Fragment deployment:** 8-16% (prefers complete sentences)
- **Contractions:** 18-26% (relaxed professional)

## Voice Authenticity Scoring

Use the 4-dimension rubric (25 points each, 100 total):

**Vocabulary (25 points):**
- 20-25: Signature phrases present, military precision, reading metaphors
- 15-19: Appropriate professional vocabulary
- 10-14: Generic military language
- 0-9: Wrong register or casual slang

**Syntax (25 points):**
- 20-25: Words/line 8-16, complete sentences dominant, compound structures
- 15-19: Slightly outside range
- 10-14: Generic structure
- 0-9: Too terse or verbose

**Rhythm (25 points):**
- 20-25: Measured, professional, structured pacing
- 15-19: Mostly controlled rhythm
- 10-14: Mixed pacing
- 0-9: Rushed or meandering

**Content (25 points):**
- 20-25: Hierarchy-aware, protective, formal-with-warmth
- 15-19: Generally appropriate
- 10-14: Generic response
- 0-9: Out-of-character

**Target Score:** 90+ for major scenes
