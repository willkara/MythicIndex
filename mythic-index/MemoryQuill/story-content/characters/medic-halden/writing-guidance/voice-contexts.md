# Medic Halden - Voice by Context

---

## Context Distribution

- **Active Treatment:** 60% (urgent, action-focused)
- **Status Report:** 25% (precise casualty counts)
- **Deferential:** 15% (acknowledging Aldwin's expertise)

---

## Active Treatment Context (60%)

**Words/line:** 7.8 avg (very short for speed)
**Key Markers:** "Already," fragments, action sequence

### Example

"Already on it! Tourniquets here, pressure there! Get water!"

---
