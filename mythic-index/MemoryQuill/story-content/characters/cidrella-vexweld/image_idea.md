# Image Ideas for Cidrella "Cid" Vexweld

## Character Summary
Adult gnome sorcerer/artificer with red copper-and-blue hair and a mechanical left arm. Partner to Korrath Threnx and co-creator of Veridian Agronomics' technology. Her prosthetic occasionally sparks with magical energy (a tell that endangered the Company's infiltration). Brilliant inventor with a complex role - creator of technology now misused.

## Proposed Image Ideas

### 1. The Mechanical Arm
**Scene:** Close-up study of Cid's mechanical left arm - the artifice, the magical integration, perhaps showing the spark that almost gave them away.
- **Pose/Action:** Arm extended, examining it or working with it, magical spark visible
- **Setting:** Workshop or close crop
- **Mood:** Innovation and vulnerability, the tell that can't be hidden
- **Tags:** close-up, artifact, magical, distinctive-feature

### 2. Inventor at Work
**Scene:** Cid in her element - surrounded by tools, components, and half-finished projects, creating something new.
- **Pose/Action:** Bent over workbench, magical tools in both hands (mechanical arm functioning perfectly)
- **Setting:** Workshop with artifice equipment
- **Mood:** Genius at work, creation in progress
- **Tags:** crafting, workshop, focused, inventive

### 3. The Spark Moment
**Scene:** The gala moment when Cid's mechanical arm sparked at exactly the wrong time, threatening to expose the infiltration.
- **Pose/Action:** Arm sparking, face showing controlled panic, trying to hide the reaction
- **Setting:** Gala setting, security potentially noticing
- **Mood:** Tension, the body betraying the mission
- **Tags:** pivotal, tension, action, problematic

### 4. Partnership with Threnx
**Scene:** Cid and Korrath working together on a project - the inventor partnership that built something now corrupted.
- **Pose/Action:** Both engaged in collaborative work, complementary skills visible
- **Setting:** Laboratory or workshop
- **Mood:** Creative partnership, the good intentions before corruption
- **Tags:** two-character, collaboration, backstory, invention

### 5. Copper-and-Blue
**Scene:** Portrait highlighting Cid's distinctive red copper-and-blue hair - the visual signature of the gnome artificer.
- **Pose/Action:** Face study, hair prominent, intelligent eyes

- **Tags:** portrait, distinctive-feature, character-study
