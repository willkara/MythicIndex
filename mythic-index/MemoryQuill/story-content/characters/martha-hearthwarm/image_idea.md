# Image Ideas for Martha Hearthwarm

## Current Imagery
- **3 images** - Need 2+ more for minimum of 5

## Character Summary
Middle-aged to elderly cook at Veridian Agronomics who unknowingly prepares meals for trafficked children, believing them to be student apprentices. Genuinely kind-hearted, proud of her work, completely manipulated by Dr. Mourngrave. Represents the human cost of the Silent Hand's deceptions - good people used for evil purposes. **Will be devastated when she learns the truth.**

## Proposed Image Ideas

### 1. Proud of "Her" Apprentices
**Scene:** Martha beaming as she speaks about preparing nutritious meals for "the young folks" - genuine care masking tragic ignorance.
- **Pose/Action:** Warm smile, hands perhaps on apron or gesturing about her cooking
- **Setting:** Kitchen or gala, speaking to someone (perhaps Aldwin)
- **Mood:** Tragic irony - genuine love in service of evil she doesn't know
- **Tags:** innocent, tragic, warm, ignorant

### 2. The Cook's Kitchen
**Scene:** Martha in her domain - preparing the nutrient-rich meals that are actually a prisoner's diet, unaware of their true purpose.
- **Pose/Action:** Cooking, tasting, adjusting - the professional at work
- **Setting:** Veridian kitchen, ingredients and pots visible
- **Mood:** Expertise applied to unknowing complicity
- **Tags:** cooking, professional, tragic-irony, domestic

### 3. Following Dr. Mourngrave's Guidance
**Scene:** Martha receiving nutritional instructions from Dr. Mourngrave, trusting completely in the woman manipulating her.
- **Pose/Action:** Listening attentively, taking notes perhaps, trusting expression
- **Setting:** Meeting area, Mourngrave's authority visible
- **Mood:** The manipulation in action, trust weaponized
- **Tags:** two-character, manipulation, trusting, tragic

### 4. Worried About Tired Children
**Scene:** Martha expressing concern that "the apprentices seem so tired lately" - her kindness noticing symptoms she can't correctly interpret.
- **Pose/Action:** Worried expression, hands clasped, speaking about concerns
- **Setting:** Kitchen or conversation
- **Mood:** Good heart seeing wrong signs, the instinct suppressed by false explanations
- **Tags:** worried, caring, tragic, innocent

### 5. The Truth Revealed (Future)
**Scene:** The devastating moment when Martha learns what she's actually been doing - the cook's world shattering.
- **Pose/Action:** Hand to mouth, tears forming, horror and grief
- **Setting:** Moment of revelation
- **Mood:** The human cost of deception, innocence destroyed
- **Tags:** emotional, devastating, tragic, pivotal
