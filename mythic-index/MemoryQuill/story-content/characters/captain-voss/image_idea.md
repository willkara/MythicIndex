## Proposed Image Ideas

- **Pose/Action:** Saber raised, horse at full gallop, squadron visible behind
- **Setting:** Battle of Westwall, attacking mercenary flank
- **Mood:** Young officer proving himself, deadly competence
- **Tags:** combat, cavalry, action, leadership

### 3. Reporting to Atlock
**Scene:** Voss delivering his squadron's report to Commander Atlock, crisp military bearing.

- **Pose/Action:** Direct gaze, confident but not arrogant, cavalry officer's bearing
- **Setting:** Neutral or military background
- **Mood:** The next generation of leadership, earned confidence
- **Tags:** portrait, professional, promising

### 5. Mirrored Charge
**Scene:** Voss and his southern squadron executing the perfectly mirrored maneuver with Jorik's northern squadron, professional excellence in formation.
- **Pose/Action:** Leading charge, aware of Jorik's parallel movement
