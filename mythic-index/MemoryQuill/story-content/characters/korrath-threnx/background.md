# Korrath "Wallbreaker" Threnx - Background

## Origin
- **Birthplace**: A dragonborn enclave with strong ties to the military and engineering guilds of Waterdeep.
- **Cultural Background**: Comes from a dragonborn culture with strong traditions of craftsmanship, honor, and military service. The Threnx name has been synonymous with masterful engineering for three generations.

## Family
- **Family Name**: Threnx
- **Family Dynamics**: Korrath's family name is a source of immense pride and pressure. He was raised on stories of his ancestors' legendary creations—fortifications that never fell, siege engines that won wars. His family expects nothing less than perfection from him, a standard he has rigorously upheld.

## History
- **Military Career**: Served as a premier specialist—the sapper and architect called upon for the most difficult fortifications during the Border Wars. His work was flawless, earning him the nickname "Wallbreaker" for his ability to analyze and overcome any defensive structure.

- **Freelance Career**: After the wars, Korrath transitioned to high-profile private contracts, designing secure vaults and impenetrable manor defenses for Waterdeep's elite. His reputation is spotless. He works frequently with Cidrella "Cid" Vexweld, whose chaotic genius in magical-technical fusion perfectly complements his methodical precision. Their partnership is known for producing work of unparalleled quality.

- **The Veridian Agronomics Contract**: Korrath and Cid were commissioned to design and build a state-of-the-art agricultural research facility in the northern wilderness. It was presented as a philanthropic endeavor to solve regional food shortages.
    - **Korrath's Contribution**: He designed the structural layout, the physical security, and the complex environmental regulation systems—a masterwork of efficiency and durability.
    - **Cid's Contribution**: She designed the sophisticated magical-technical systems for irrigation, climate control, and power, all seamlessly integrated into Korrath's framework.
    - **The Deception**: The project was a resounding success, lauded as one of their finest achievements. They had no knowledge of its true, sinister purpose.

- **The Revelation**: The truth of the facility's use will be brought to him by the Last Light Company. This event will mark the first time in his family's history that a Threnx creation has been used for such a monstrous purpose. It will be a devastating blow to his professional honor and personal identity, forcing him to confront the unforeseen consequences of his work and setting him on a new path.

## Personal Details
- **Hobbies**: He is a dedicated breeder of ornamental lizards and has a deep fascination with landscape art and architecture—a love for structures that bring beauty to the world, not just security.
- **Favorite Meal**: Stone-grilled river trout served with precision-cut root vegetables and a drizzle of basilisk-bone reduction. He plates it with draftsman's care, claiming symmetry makes flavor "structurally sound."
- **Favorite Drink**: Chilled jade tea shaken with lime and a shard of glacial ice. He times each shake to a metronome, enjoying the engineered consistency of the ritual.
