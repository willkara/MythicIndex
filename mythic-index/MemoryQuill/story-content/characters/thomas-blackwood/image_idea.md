# Image Ideas for Thomas Blackwood

## Current Imagery
- **4 images** - Need 1+ more for minimum of 5

## Character Summary
19-year-old jeweler's apprentice with reddish-brown hair. Son of Harriet Blackwood, fiancé to Lily. Subject of a "rescue" that turned out to be a false alarm - he was simply visiting his betrothed, not in danger. Represents the ordinary people the Company protects and the occasional awkward outcome of heroic intervention.

## Proposed Image Ideas

### 1. The Embarrassed Rescuee
**Scene:** Thomas's face when he realizes the Last Light Company came to "rescue" him from visiting his fiancée.
- **Pose/Action:** Expression of confused embarrassment, hand perhaps rubbing neck
- **Setting:** Village, Company visible, Lily nearby amused
- **Mood:** Comedy of errors, the awkward outcome of good intentions
- **Tags:** humorous, embarrassed, character-moment

### 2. Young Craftsman
**Scene:** Thomas at his jeweler's bench, working with the precision his apprenticeship is teaching him.
- **Pose/Action:** Bent over work, tools in hand, focused concentration
- **Setting:** Jeweler's workshop, small detailed work
- **Mood:** Youth learning craft, the next generation of skill
- **Tags:** craftsmanship, apprentice, focused, professional

### 3. Reddish-Brown Youth
**Scene:** Portrait of Thomas showing his distinctive reddish-brown hair and the youth that made his mother worry.
- **Pose/Action:** Direct gaze, youthful features, hopeful expression
- **Setting:** Neutral or workshop background
- **Mood:** The ordinary person worth protecting, community's future
- **Tags:** portrait, youth, hopeful

### 4. With Lily
**Scene:** Thomas and Lily together - the relationship that caused all the confusion, young love that reads as innocent happiness.
- **Pose/Action:** Standing together, comfortable intimacy, perhaps she's teasing him
- **Setting:** Village, domestic
- **Mood:** Love worth protecting, the happiness ordinary life offers
- **Tags:** two-character, romance, warm, happy

### 5. Grateful Despite Embarrassment
**Scene:** Thomas thanking the Company despite the awkwardness - acknowledging they came because they cared.
- **Pose/Action:** Offering handshake or bow, sincere gratitude beneath embarrassment
- **Setting:** Village, Company members present
- **Mood:** Grace in awkward situation, understanding beneath humor
- **Tags:** emotional, grateful, character-moment, community
