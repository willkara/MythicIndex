# Image Ideas for Master Korwin Ironwall

## Current Imagery
- **3 images** - Need 2+ more for minimum of 5

## Character Summary
Dwarven master mason and Guild Master with weathered craftsman's appearance. Old friend/rival of Grimjaw. Arrives with Guild support after the Battle of Westwall. Recognizes Grimjaw's work as mastercraft despite their old rivalry. Values practical craft over pretty designs.

---

## Proposed Image Ideas

### 1. Old Rivals Reunited
**Scene:** Master Korwin greeting Grimjaw after the battle - the mischievous delight of an old rival seeing quality work.
- **Pose/Action:** Clasping Grimjaw's arm or shoulder, knowing grin, competitive respect
- **Setting:** Damaged Westwall, assessing the fortifications
- **Mood:** Professional respect transcending rivalry, old friends reunited through craft
- **Tags:** two-character, reunion, professional, warm

### 2. Assessing the Damage
**Scene:** Korwin examining Westwall's structural damage with the expert eye of a master mason.
- **Pose/Action:** Running hand along damaged wall, reading the stone
- **Setting:** Battle-damaged fortification
- **Mood:** The professional assessing work to be done, expertise in action
- **Tags:** assessment, professional, expert, serious

### 3. Guild Master's Authority
**Scene:** Portrait of Korwin showing the weathered face and bearing of a dwarf who's earned his title through decades of stone work.
- **Pose/Action:** Direct gaze, perhaps arms crossed, guild authority evident
- **Setting:** Neutral or stone background
- **Mood:** Earned authority, the master mason's presence
- **Tags:** portrait, authoritative, experienced

### 4. Offering Guild Support
**Scene:** Korwin formally offering the Mason's Guild's assistance in rebuilding - professional solidarity in action.
- **Pose/Action:** Speaking to Company leaders, gesturing at work to be done, offering help
- **Setting:** Westwall courtyard, guild members visible behind
- **Mood:** Community responding to crisis, guilds supporting heroes
- **Tags:** offer, professional, community, supportive

### 5. "That's Mastercraft"
**Scene:** Korwin examining something Grimjaw built, expression shifting from critical to impressed against his will.
- **Pose/Action:** Inspecting work closely, eyebrows rising, grudging admiration forming
- **Setting:** Examining fortification or repair
- **Mood:** Rivalry acknowledging excellence, professional honesty
- **Tags:** assessment, impressed, two-character-implied, professional
