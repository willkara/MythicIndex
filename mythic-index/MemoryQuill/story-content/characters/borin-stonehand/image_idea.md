# Image Ideas for Borin Stonehand

## Current Imagery
- **4 images** - Need 1+ more for minimum of 5

## Character Summary
Middle-aged dwarf cleric with copper-red beard featuring a central braid. Provides divine healing and spiritual guidance to the Company. Represents faith in action - not preachy but practical. The healer who prays with his hands as much as his words.

---

## Proposed Image Ideas

### 1. Healing Light
**Scene:** Borin channeling divine healing energy, hands glowing with holy power as he treats a wounded comrade.
- **Pose/Action:** Hands on patient, face lifted in prayer, golden light emanating
- **Setting:** Field treatment or medical area
- **Mood:** Faith made manifest, practical divinity
- **Tags:** healing, magic, divine, action

### 2. The Copper-Red Braid
**Scene:** Portrait emphasizing Borin's distinctive beard - the copper-red color with careful central braid showing dwarven pride.
- **Pose/Action:** Face forward or slight angle, beard prominent, expression wise and kind
- **Setting:** Neutral or chapel background
- **Mood:** The face of comfort and strength, faith personified
- **Tags:** portrait, distinctive-feature, character-study

### 3. Prayer Before Battle
**Scene:** Borin blessing the Company before a dangerous mission, divine protection being invoked.
- **Pose/Action:** Arms raised in blessing, Company members bowed or standing, light suggesting divine presence
- **Setting:** Pre-mission gathering, dawn or evening light
- **Mood:** Faith fortifying courage, community in prayer
- **Tags:** blessing, group-scene, spiritual, dramatic

### 4. Quiet Counsel
**Scene:** Borin in private conversation with a troubled Company member, offering wisdom without judgment.
- **Pose/Action:** Seated close to the other person, listening posture, patient expression
- **Setting:** Quiet corner, intimate setting
- **Mood:** The pastoral role, faith as comfort
- **Tags:** intimate, counseling, two-character, caring

### 5. Dwarven Determination
**Scene:** Borin in a moment of stubborn dwarven resolve - refusing to leave a patient, standing ground on principle.
- **Pose/Action:** Feet planted, chin up, the immovable object
- **Setting:** Crisis situation, pressure to abandon position
- **Mood:** Faith as bedrock, the healer who doesn't retreat
- **Tags:** determination, character-moment, strong
