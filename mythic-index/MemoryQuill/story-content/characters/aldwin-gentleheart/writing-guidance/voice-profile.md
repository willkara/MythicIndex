# Aldwin Gentleheart - Voice Profile

**Character Role:** Healer, Philosopher
**Core Voice:** Gentle questions | Philosophical comfort | Tea ceremony rhythm

## Quantitative Profile

Based on 650+ dialogue instances analyzed from actual story content (phase3 analysis).

### Statistical Metrics

- **Average words/line:** 13.7 (Range: 3-45, Median: 12, StdDev: 7.2)
- **Contraction frequency:** 8% (very low - formal, gentle speech)
- **Fragment usage:** 6% (very low - complete thoughts preferred)
- **Question ratio:** 47% (very high - signature teaching/guiding method)
- **Sentence complexity:** Simple 28%, Compound 34%, Complex 38%

### Target Ranges for Authentic Voice

- **Acceptable words/line:** 10-20 (within 2 standard deviations, longest character)
- **Contractions:** 4-12% (very formal, preserve distance)
- **Fragment deployment:** 3-9% (minimal - complete thoughts)
- **Questions:** 40-55% (highest of all characters - interrogative as guidance method)

## Signature Phrases

| Phrase | Usage | Frequency | Notes |
|--------|-------|-----------|-------|
| "Shall I prepare tea?" | Offering comfort | 9 instances | **ONLY Aldwin** - ritual invitation |
| "Shall we..." | Gentle invitation | 12 instances | Collaborative framing |
| "Easy now, easy..." | Calming repetition | 14 instances | Medical/crisis reassurance (context: humans/animals) |
| "May I...?" | Permission request | 7 instances | Respectful approach |
| "The journey" / "passage" | Euphemism for death | 6 instances | Gentle terminology |
| "What do you feel/need?" | Assessment questions | 11 instances | Guiding inquiry |

**Minimum Deployment:** Major scenes should include at least 1 signature phrase, especially "Shall I prepare tea?" or "Easy now, easy..." in comfort contexts.

## Character-Specific Lexicon

### Nature/Garden Metaphors
- "roots" / "growing" / "seasons" / "blooming" / "withering"
- Organic growth metaphors for healing/philosophy

### Water/Flow Language
- "rivers" / "streams" / "currents" / "tides" / "flow"
- Movement and natural processes

### Journey/Path Terminology
- "crossroads" / "passages" / "roads" / "path"
- Life progress and death euphemisms

### Gentle Light Language
- "dawn" / "dusk" / "twilight" / "gentle light"
- Never harsh or bright - softness paramount

### Absence of Modern Medical Terms
- Avoid: "infection," "antibiotic," "sutures"
- Prefer: "wound," "healing," "tending," "salve"

## Voice Authenticity Scoring

Use the 4-dimension rubric (25 points each, 100 total):

**Vocabulary (25 points):**
- 20-25: Signature phrases present, metaphorical language, philosophical depth
- 15-19: Appropriate gentle vocabulary
- 10-14: Generic comfort language
- 0-9: Harsh or modern clinical terms

**Syntax (25 points):**
- 20-25: Words/line 10-20, high question ratio (40%+), complete thoughts
- 15-19: Slightly outside range, mostly questions
- 10-14: Generic structure
- 0-9: Too terse or overly complex

**Rhythm (25 points):**
- 20-25: Measured, gentle, contemplative, philosophical
- 15-19: Mostly measured, occasional rushed feeling
- 10-14: Mixed pacing
- 0-9: Harsh or abrupt

**Content (25 points):**
- 20-25: Philosophical depth, healing intent, comfort-focused
- 15-19: Appropriately gentle
- 10-14: Generic support
- 0-9: Harsh, dismissive, or clinical coldness

**Target Score:** 90+ for major scenes, 85+ for minor
