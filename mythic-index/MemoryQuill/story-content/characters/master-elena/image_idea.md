# Image Ideas for Master Elena

## Current Imagery
- **4 images** - Need 1+ more for minimum of 5

## Character Summary
Human master carpenter in her late 60s-70s with silver hair and weathered hands. Guild master representing decades of craft expertise. Her hands tell the story of a life building things that last. Works with wood like others speak a language.

---

## Proposed Image Ideas

### 1. The Craftsman's Hands
**Scene:** Close-up of Master Elena's weathered hands working wood - every scar and callus a chapter of expertise.
- **Pose/Action:** Hands planing wood or fitting a joint, tools extensions of flesh
- **Setting:** Workshop, good lighting on hands
- **Mood:** A lifetime of craft made visible, expertise in every mark
- **Tags:** close-up, hands, craftsmanship, detail

### 2. Silver Hair and Sawdust
**Scene:** Portrait of Elena in her workshop - silver hair perhaps catching sawdust, the master in her element.
- **Pose/Action:** Working or assessing a piece, natural workshop environment
- **Setting:** Carpenter's workshop, tools and wood around
- **Mood:** Where she belongs, the workspace as extension of self
- **Tags:** portrait, workshop, professional, authentic

### 3. Teaching the Craft
**Scene:** Master Elena instructing a younger carpenter, demonstrating technique, passing on tradition.
- **Pose/Action:** Showing how it's done, student watching closely
- **Setting:** Workshop, demonstration in progress
- **Mood:** Legacy through teaching, craft preserved
- **Tags:** teaching, two-character, tradition, mentorship

### 4. Assessing the Work
**Scene:** Elena examining a piece of carpentry - the master's eye finding every flaw and virtue.
- **Pose/Action:** Holding wood to light, running finger along grain, critical assessment
- **Setting:** Workshop or site
- **Mood:** Standards maintained, the eye that sees truth in wood
- **Tags:** assessment, professional, critical, expert

### 5. Building That Lasts
**Scene:** Elena overseeing a construction project - her work visible in something that will outlast her.
- **Pose/Action:** Directing workers or inspecting structure, pride in enduring work
- **Setting:** Construction site, something substantial being built
- **Mood:** Legacy in wood and stone, craft that serves generations
- **Tags:** construction, leadership, legacy, professional

---

## Visual Consistency Notes
- Human female, late 60s-70s
- Silver hair
- Weathered hands (distinctive feature, tells her story)
- Carpenter/woodworker's practical attire
- May have sawdust in hair or on clothes
- Expression: Focused, assessing, occasionally warm pride
- Body language: Comfortable with tools, reads wood naturally
- Guild master representing craft traditions
