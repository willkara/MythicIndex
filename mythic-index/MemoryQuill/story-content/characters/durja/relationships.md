# Durja - Relationships

## Professional Relationships
- **The Last Light Company**:
  - *Nature of Relationship*: Innkeeper and patrons/temporary residents.
  - *Hierarchy*: She runs the inn, they are her customers.
  - *Dynamics*: Values their payment and their ability to keep the peace. Observes their actions and reputation.
  - *History*: First encountered at The Wayward Compass Inn.
  - *Current Status*: Professional relationship, with a growing awareness of their influence.
  - *Professional Opinion of*: Sees them as effective, if sometimes mysterious, peacekeepers.

- **Inn Patrons**:
  - *Nature of Relationship*: Innkeeper and customers.
  - *Hierarchy*: She serves them.
  - *Dynamics*: Manages their needs, listens to their gossip, and maintains order.
  - *History*: Ongoing.
  - *Current Status*: Regular business relationships.
  - *Professional Opinion of*: Views them pragmatically as sources of income and information.
