# Image Ideas for Quierk

## Current Imagery
- **1 image:** Tavern portrait holding wooden tankard, owl-like features, leather apron

## Character Summary
Aarakocra innkeeper of The Windborne Roost and City Watch intelligence asset. Great horned owl features with russet/tan/cream plumage. "A creature of the sky who runs his inn with grounded efficiency." Worked with Lady Moira Blackwater for years. Misses nothing, communicates through gesture when words aren't needed.

---

## Proposed Image Ideas

### 1. Aerial Surveillance
**Scene:** Quierk in flight above the northern road to his inn, golden-amber eyes scanning the landscape below for approaching travelers or threats.
- **Pose/Action:** Wings fully extended in a silent glide, head turned downward, talons tucked
- **Setting:** Dawn or dusk sky over the Thornwood edge, road visible far below
- **Mood:** Watchful freedom, the spy who sees all, sky creature in his element
- **Tags:** flight, surveillance, aerial-view, dramatic

### 2. Silent Assessment
**Scene:** Quierk standing in the doorway of The Windborne Roost, head cocked to one side, assessing new arrivals with a single calculating glance.
- **Pose/Action:** Arms loosely at sides, feather ear-tufts alert, eyes narrowed slightly
- **Setting:** Inn entrance, warm light spilling from behind him, travelers' shadows approaching
- **Mood:** Quiet menace beneath hospitality, seeing through covers and lies
- **Tags:** doorway-portrait, assessment, intelligence-asset, warm-cool-contrast

### 3. Discreet Exchange
**Scene:** Quierk passing a small folded note to someone while appearing to simply refill their drink, his expression neutral and professional.
- **Pose/Action:** One hand tilting a pitcher, the other subtly passing paper beneath the table
- **Setting:** Busy tavern, other patrons in soft focus, intimate moment hidden in plain sight
- **Mood:** Tradecraft, the spy's art, secrets exchanged openly
- **Tags:** spy-craft, subtle, two-character, covert

### 4. Night Watch from the Roost
**Scene:** Quierk perched on the roof peak of his inn at twilight, silhouetted against a purple sky, keeping vigil over the road.
- **Pose/Action:** Wings folded tight, hunched forward on the roof ridge, head slowly turning
- **Setting:** Inn roof at dusk, first stars appearing, road stretching into darkness
- **Mood:** Guardian in shadow, the watcher no one notices, eternal vigilance
- **Tags:** silhouette, rooftop, twilight, atmospheric

### 5. The Knowing Glance
**Scene:** Close-up portrait of Quierk's face as he recognizes someone (like Lyra) despite their disguise - a subtle curve of his beak suggesting professional amusement.
- **Pose/Action:** Head tilted, golden eyes holding contact, the faintest hint of acknowledgment
- **Setting:** Tight crop, warm tavern lighting catching the texture of his plumage
- **Mood:** "I see you," competence recognizing competence, unspoken understanding
- **Tags:** close-up, recognition, subtle-expression, intelligence

### 6. Gesture Without Words
**Scene:** Quierk meeting the exhausted Last Light Company upon their return, communicating sanctuary and support through silent gestures alone.
- **Pose/Action:** One wing partially extended toward the inn, the other hand gesturing "come," no words needed
- **Setting:** Edge of clearing at dawn, weary riders approaching, inn warm behind him
- **Mood:** Sanctuary offered, professional compassion, the value of silence
- **Tags:** welcoming, dawn-scene, gesture, multiple-characters

## Visual Consistency Notes
- Great horned owl features: prominent feather ear-tufts, sharp curved beak
- Plumage: mottled russet, tan, and cream; fluffy cream chest feathers
- Wings with mossy green coverts and cream primary feathers
- Piercing golden-amber eyes with sharp, contracted pupils
- Practical innkeeper attire: green tunic, worn brown leather apron
- Talon-tipped hands with rough, weathered skin
- Body language: still, economical, observant
