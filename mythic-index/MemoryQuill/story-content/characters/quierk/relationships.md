## Relationship Dynamics

### Trust Model
Quierk operates on a tiered trust system:
1. **Full Trust**: Lady Moira, proven Watch operatives
2. **Operational Trust**: Last Light Company (vouched by Thorne)
3. **Professional Distance**: Regular customers, traveling merchants
4. **Assessment Mode**: New arrivals (constantly evaluating)

### Communication Style
- **With Trusted Contacts**: Direct but economical; says what's needed
- **With Operatives**: Supportive actions over words; gestures toward safety
- **With Unknowns**: Careful probing through seemingly innocent questions
- **Under Stress**: Defaults to silence and action [FIRM ch32]

### Loyalty Structure
His loyalties appear to be:
1. **The Watch / Lady Moira**: Years of faithful service
2. **His establishment**: The Roost is his life's work
3. **Operational success**: Wants missions to succeed
4. **Personal survival**: "A survivor" suggests self-preservation instinct
