*   **Permanently Maimed**: In the battle's climax, his sword arm was brutally crushed and broken by Vera in her feral hybrid form. The injury is permanent and career-ending, destroying his identity as a warrior.
*   **Broken and Captured**: Defeated, maimed, and with his mercenary force shattered, he was captured by Commander Atlock's cavalry. His reputation as a formidable commander is in ruins.
