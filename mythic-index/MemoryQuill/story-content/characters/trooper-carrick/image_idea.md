# Image Ideas for Trooper Carrick

## Current Imagery
- **1 image:** Wounded soldier portrait with lantern, severe bloody torso wound, chiaroscuro lighting

## Character Summary
Middle-aged City Watch scout who returns badly wounded from reconnaissance. Most seriously injured of the returning scouts - a blade got under his mail. Too wounded to fight in the Battle of Westwall, he serves as a runner for the medical team. Represents the cost of duty and quiet courage.

---

## Proposed Image Ideas

### 1. The Desperate Ride
**Scene:** Carrick barely staying in his saddle, slumped forward and clutching his bleeding side, racing back to Westwall with urgent news.
- **Pose/Action:** One hand gripping the reins, other pressed to his wound, face contorted with pain and determination
- **Setting:** Forest road at speed, horse lathered, dust and motion blur
- **Mood:** Desperation, duty above self, the cost of being first to know
- **Tags:** action, horseback, wounded, dramatic-motion

### 2. Runner in the Chaos
**Scene:** Carrick limping through the battle at Westwall, carrying water and supplies to the healers despite his own injuries.
- **Pose/Action:** Hunched but moving, arms full of medical supplies, face set with grim determination
- **Setting:** Courtyard during battle, smoke and movement around him, makeshift medical station ahead
- **Mood:** Service in any capacity, courage without glory, finding purpose when sidelined
- **Tags:** battle-scene, support-role, determination, chaos

### 3. Before the Wound
**Scene:** Carrick on patrol before the fateful scouting mission, alert and professional, lantern held high against the forest darkness.
- **Pose/Action:** Mounted or standing watch, lantern raised, scanning the treeline with practiced eyes
- **Setting:** Forest edge at dusk, shadows lengthening, the calm before disaster
- **Mood:** Professional vigilance, the ordinary moment before everything changes
- **Tags:** patrol, pre-battle, lantern, atmospheric

### 4. Under the Healer's Hands
**Scene:** Carrick being tended by Aldwin and Halden, showing gratitude and exhaustion as they work on his side wound.
- **Pose/Action:** Lying or sitting, shirt open to reveal wound, face showing relief and pain
- **Setting:** Medical station in Westwall courtyard, healers' hands visible working
- **Mood:** Vulnerability, being cared for, survival
- **Tags:** medical-scene, two-character, vulnerable, warm

### 5. Wounded Pride
**Scene:** The moment Carrick is told he's off the line - showing his conflicted relief mixed with the sting of being unable to fight alongside his comrades.
- **Pose/Action:** Standing with hand pressed to bandaged ribs, eyes downcast, shoulders slightly slumped
- **Setting:** Westwall courtyard before battle, other soldiers preparing in background
- **Mood:** Complex emotion - relief and shame, acceptance of limits
- **Tags:** emotional-portrait, pre-battle, internal-conflict

### 6. The Scout's Return
**Scene:** The moment Carrick and the other wounded scouts thunder into the Westwall courtyard - the alarm raised, the news delivered.
- **Pose/Action:** Barely staying upright in saddle, arm raised weakly, calling out warning
- **Setting:** Courtyard gates, people rushing toward the riders
- **Mood:** Mission accomplished at great cost, the messenger's burden
- **Tags:** arrival, dramatic-moment, group-scene, urgent

## Visual Consistency Notes
- Middle-aged (late 40s-50s), lean and wiry frame
- Weather-beaten, ruddy complexion with deep facial lines
- Pale blue-grey eyes, deep-set and weary
- Salt-and-pepper hair and stubble, mostly hidden by helmet
- Tarnished steel morion helmet with central ridge
- Bronzed-steel breastplate with wear marks
- Dark fabric tunic beneath armor
- Expression: grim, resolute, enduring
