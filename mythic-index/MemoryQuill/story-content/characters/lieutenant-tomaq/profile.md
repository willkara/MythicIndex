# Lieutenant Tomaq - Profile

## Basic Information
- **Full Name**: Lieutenant Tomaq
- **Race**: Orc
- **Role**: City Watch Lieutenant stationed at Westwall Watchpost
- **First Appearance**: Chapter 15 - Black Hawks Strike
- **Status**: Active

## Physical Description
- **Height/Build**: Towering frame typical of orcs
- **Eyes**: One-eyed (lost eye in previous service)
- **Distinguishing Features**: Eye patch or scar from lost eye, imposing military bearing
- **Typical Clothing**: City Watch uniform with lieutenant's insignia
- **Body Language**: Professional military posture, by-the-books demeanor

## Personality
- **Archetype**: By-the-books Military Professional
- **Temperament**: Strict, procedural, disciplined
- **Positive Traits**: Professional, reliable, follows protocol
- **Negative Traits**: Inflexible, bureaucratic, suspicious of non-standard operations
- **Moral Alignment**: Lawful Neutral (follows rules and chain of command)

## Role & Relationships
- **Professional Role**: City Watch Lieutenant, Westwall Watchpost commander
- **Relationship to Main Characters**: Contemporary of Thorne Brightward from City Watch
- **Professional Dynamic**: Grudging respect for Company effectiveness despite protocol concerns
- **Authority Position**: Reports to City Watch superiors, must balance local autonomy with chain of command

## Notes
- Represents official City Watch perspective and bureaucratic constraints
- Professional respect for Thorne despite disagreement over methods
- Torn between local effectiveness and official protocol requirements
- Acts as grounded counterpoint to Company's semi-independent operations
