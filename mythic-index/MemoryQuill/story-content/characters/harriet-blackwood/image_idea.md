## Character Summary
48-year-old Millbridge matron with dark hair streaked with gray. Thomas's mother. Represents the village community the Company protects. The worried parent, grateful friend, and practical village leader who manages crisis with calm efficiency.

## Proposed Image Ideas

### 1. Mother's Worry
**Scene:** Harriet's face when she learns Thomas might be in danger - the visceral fear of a parent for their child.
- **Pose/Action:** Hand to heart or mouth, eyes wide, the moment before action
- **Setting:** Village setting, news arriving
- **Mood:** The universal parental fear, love expressed through worry
- **Tags:** emotional, parent, worried, relatable

### 2. Village Matriarch
**Scene:** Harriet in her element - organizing village response to crisis, directing neighbors with practical competence.
- **Pose/Action:** Gesturing direction, giving calm instructions, people responding
- **Setting:** Village square or home, crisis being managed
- **Mood:** Leadership through experience, community strength
- **Tags:** leadership, community, practical, strong

### 3. Gray-Streaked Wisdom
**Scene:** Portrait of Harriet showing the dark hair streaked with gray - the visual mark of a life of work and worry.
- **Pose/Action:** Direct gaze, calm expression, strength visible in features
- **Setting:** Neutral or home background
- **Mood:** Earned wisdom, the face that holds families together
- **Tags:** portrait, character-study, maternal

### 4. Grateful Welcome
**Scene:** Harriet greeting the Company with the gratitude of someone whose worst fears didn't materialize.
- **Pose/Action:** Clasping hands with Company member, relief and thanks evident
- **Setting:** Village, after Thomas is confirmed safe
- **Mood:** The community's gratitude personified
- **Tags:** emotional, grateful, two-character, warm

### 5. Everyday Strength
**Scene:** Harriet in daily life - the ordinary moments of a village matron that build the foundation communities rest on.
- **Pose/Action:** Working at practical task, the competence of routine
- **Setting:** Home or village
- **Mood:** The heroism of ordinary people, strength in the everyday
- **Tags:** domestic, strong, everyday, grounded
