# Image Ideas for Master Aldara

## Current Imagery
- **4 images** - Need 1+ more for minimum of 5

## Character Summary
Elderly halfling master healer with a stern weathered face. Represents the highest level of healing expertise. Strict but dedicated, she maintains exacting standards because lives depend on them. The teacher and master healer whose approval must be earned.

---

## Proposed Image Ideas

### 1. Exacting Standards
**Scene:** Master Aldara correcting a student's technique with her stern gaze - standards maintained because they matter.
- **Pose/Action:** Standing over student, pointing out error, expression stern but instructive
- **Setting:** Training or medical area
- **Mood:** Excellence demanded, lives depending on getting it right
- **Tags:** teaching, stern, professional, exacting

### 2. Weathered Wisdom
**Scene:** Portrait capturing Master Aldara's stern weathered face - decades of healing written in every line.
- **Pose/Action:** Direct gaze, uncompromising expression, healer's hands visible
- **Setting:** Neutral or medical background
- **Mood:** Earned authority, the master's presence
- **Tags:** portrait, stern, experienced, character-study

### 3. Halfling Among Tall Folk
**Scene:** Master Aldara commanding respect despite her small stature - perhaps directing treatment of a much larger patient.
- **Pose/Action:** Standing on step or platform, directing with absolute authority
- **Setting:** Medical crisis, larger figures following her orders
- **Mood:** Authority transcends size, competence commands respect
- **Tags:** authority, size-contrast, commanding, professional

### 4. Gentle Hands, Stern Eyes
**Scene:** The contrast of Aldara's gentle healing touch with her stern expression - caring shown through action not softness.
- **Pose/Action:** Hands working delicately on patient, face maintaining serious focus
- **Setting:** Treatment area, close enough to see contrast
- **Mood:** Love expressed through excellence, not sentiment
- **Tags:** healing, contrast, professional, caring

### 5. The Master's Approval
**Scene:** A rare moment of Master Aldara showing approval - perhaps a nod or brief word to a student who got it right.
- **Pose/Action:** Slight nod, brief softening of expression, hard-won acknowledgment
- **Setting:** After successful treatment or test
- **Mood:** The validation that means everything, earned respect
- **Tags:** emotional, approval, subtle, meaningful

## Visual Consistency Notes
- Elderly halfling female
- Stern weathered face (signature feature)
- Master healer's attire, practical and clean
- Small stature but commanding presence
- Expression: Predominantly stern, rarely softens
- Body language: Precise, authoritative, unwavering standards
- Represents the highest level of healing expertise
- Approval must be earned and is rarely given easily
