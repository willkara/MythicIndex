# Veyra Thornwake - Voice Profile

**Character Role:** Protagonist, Commander
**Core Voice:** Ultra-economical | Protective | Haunted | Softens only with deep trust

## Quantitative Profile

Based on 650+ dialogue instances analyzed from actual story content (phase3 analysis).

### Statistical Metrics

- **Average words/line:** 7.2 (Range: 1-24, Median: 6, StdDev: 5.1)
- **Contraction frequency:** 18% (moderate)
- **Fragment usage:** 34% (very high - deliberate economy, highest among all characters)
- **Question ratio:** 11% (low - commands/statements dominant)
- **Sentence complexity:** Simple 68%, Compound 19%, Complex 13%

### Target Ranges for Authentic Voice

- **Acceptable words/line:** 5-11 (within 2 standard deviations)
- **Fragment deployment:** 30-38% (maintain deliberate economy)
- **Contractions:** 15-22% (moderate range)

## Voice Authenticity Scoring

Use the 4-dimension rubric (25 points each, 100 total):

**Vocabulary (25 points):**
- 20-25: Signature phrases present, lexicon accurate
- 15-19: Appropriate vocabulary, minor deviations
- 10-14: Generic but plausible
- 0-9: Wrong register or out-of-character terms

**Syntax (25 points):**
- 20-25: Words/line within target range (5-11), fragment usage 30-38%
- 15-19: Slightly outside range but recognizable
- 10-14: Generic sentence structure
- 0-9: Verbose or wrong complexity

**Rhythm (25 points):**
- 20-25: Clipped, economical, deliberate pacing
- 15-19: Mostly economical with occasional wordiness
- 10-14: Mixed pacing
- 0-9: Verbose, meandering

**Content (25 points):**
- 20-25: Appropriate emotional context, relationship-specific
- 15-19: Generally appropriate
- 10-14: Generic emotional response
- 0-9: Out-of-character emotion or response

**Target Score:** 90+ for major scenes, 85+ for minor appearances
