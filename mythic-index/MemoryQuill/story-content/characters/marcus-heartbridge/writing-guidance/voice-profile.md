# Marcus Heartbridge - Voice Profile

**Character Role:** Diplomatic Administrator
**Core Voice:** Formal hedging | Bureaucratic precision | Apologetic deference

## Quantitative Profile

Based on 650+ dialogue instances analyzed from actual story content (phase3 analysis).

### Statistical Metrics

- **Average words/line:** 17.3 (LONGEST - formal bureaucratic)
- **Contraction frequency:** 6% (LOWEST - very formal)
- **Fragment usage:** 4% (LOWEST - complete sentences)
- **Question ratio:** 22% (moderate - seeking clarification/permission)
- **Hedging language:** Very high - "may have," "certain," "particular," "expedited"
- **Apologetic tone:** Frequent - "I apologize," "unfortunately," "I regret"

### Target Ranges for Authentic Voice

- **Acceptable words/line:** 14-22 (within 2 standard deviations, very long)
- **Contractions:** 2-10% (very formal, bureaucratic)
- **Fragment deployment:** 1-7% (almost none)
- **Questions:** 18-26% (seeking clarification typical)

## Voice Authenticity Scoring

Use the 4-dimension rubric (25 points each, 100 total):

**Vocabulary (25 points):**
- 20-25: Bureaucratic terminology, hedging language, apologetic tone
- 15-19: Formal administrative language
- 10-14: Generic formal speech
- 0-9: Casual or wrong register

**Syntax (25 points):**
- 20-25: Words/line 14-22, minimal contractions (2-10%), complete sentences
- 15-19: Slightly outside range
- 10-14: Generic structure
- 0-9: Too casual or too terse

**Rhythm (25 points):**
- 20-25: Careful, measured, deliberate pacing, bureaucratic formality
- 15-19: Mostly formal
- 10-14: Mixed pacing
- 0-9: Rushed or inappropriate

**Content (25 points):**
- 20-25: Apologetic, hedging, deferential, bureaucratic anxiety
- 15-19: Generally appropriate formality
- 10-14: Generic administrator
- 0-9: Out-of-character

**Target Score:** 85+ for major scenes (lower threshold due to character's anxious nature)
