# Sergeant Kelen - Voice Red Flags

---

## Critical Red Flags

### ❌ Skipping Safety Checks

**Problem:** Kelen prioritizes safety and equipment maintenance.

**Violation:** Kelen dialogue that ignores equipment or safety.

**Correct:** Include equipment checks, safety protocols.

---

### ❌ Harsh With Subordinates

**Problem:** Kelen is professional authority, not mean.

**Violation:** Cruel or degrading language toward soldiers.

**Correct:** Firm but fair: "That's not procedure. Do it right."

---

### ❌ Careless With Equipment References

**Problem:** Equipment is constant Kelen focus.

**Violation:** Kelen scenes with zero equipment mention.

**Correction:** Include rifle, pack, ammunition, boots, straps, etc.

---

### ❌ Missing "Right Then" or "As You Were"

**Problem:** Signature phrases identify Kelen.

**Violation:** Major Kelen scenes without these signatures.

**Correction:** "Right then" for transitions, "As you were" for dismissal.

---

## Moderate Red Flags

### ⚠️ Over 12 Words/Line

**Problem:** Kelen is 9.7 avg - efficient and direct.

**Violation:** 15+ word sentences from Kelen.

**Correction:** Keep professional but concise.

---

### ⚠️ Overly Casual

**Problem:** Kelen is formal military NCO.

**Violation:** Casual slang or overly familiar tone.

**Correct:** Professional military bearing.

---

## Diagnostic Checklist

- [ ] Equipment/safety focus evident?
- [ ] "Right then" or "As you were" present?
- [ ] Professional military authority (not harsh)?
- [ ] 8-12 words/line typical?
- [ ] Equipment terminology included (rifle, pack, etc)?
- [ ] Safety protocols referenced?
- [ ] Veteran experience implied?

---

## Quick Fixes

| Issue | Fix |
|-------|-----|
| No equipment focus | Add: "rifle," "pack," "ammunition," "secure" |
| Missing signatures | "Right then" for transitions, "As you were" for dismissal |
| Too harsh | Replace cruelty with firm professionalism |
| Too wordy | Trim to 9-12 words for NCO efficiency |
| Skipping safety | Include: equipment checks, protocols, security |
