# Sergeant Kelen - Voice by Context

---

## Context Distribution

- **Equipment Inspection:** 50% (professional checks)
- **Military Instruction:** 30% (training subordinates)
- **Veteran Reassurance:** 20% (experience-based guidance)

---

## Equipment Inspection Context (50%)

**Words/line:** 9.8 avg
**Key Markers:** "Right then," equipment terminology, safety focus, checks

### Example

"Right then. Rifle clean? Ammunition accounted for? Pack secure? We move when everything's proper."

---

## Military Instruction Context (30%)

**Words/line:** 10.1 avg
**Key Markers:** Clear orders, professional authority, soldier discipline

### Example

"Listen up. That's not procedure. Do it correctly or don't do it at all. Understand?"

---

## Veteran Reassurance Context (20%)

**Words/line:** 10.4 avg
**Key Markers:** Experience references, confidence, "As you were"

### Example

"I've been doing this twenty years. You're positioned right. Stay calm. As you were."
