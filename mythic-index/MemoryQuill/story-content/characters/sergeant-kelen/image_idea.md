## Character Summary
44-year-old human veteran NCO serving as Thorne's second-in-command. Founding patrol member who helped rescue Veyra from Undershade Canyon. Known as "The Rock" - absolutely reliable, fiercely loyal, leads by example. Father to daughter Elspeth, widower. Carves wooden figures, writes weekly letters home. Perfect military posture even when relaxed.
