## Speech Patterns

- **Vocabulary Level**: Sophisticated, educated, uses political terminology naturally
- **Accent/Dialect**: Upper-class Waterdhavian, refined aristocratic pronunciation
- **Formal/Informal**: Always formal in public, calculated informality as political tool
- **Unique Phrases**: References to "family honor," "proper channels," "unfortunate necessities"
- **Verbal Tics**: Careful word choice, strategic pauses, speaks in measured tones

## Common Expressions

- **Greetings**: "Good day" or formal titles, uses names to assert familiarity or distance
- **Farewells**: "I trust this matter will be handled appropriately"
- **Deflections**: "That would be... unwise" or "There are proper channels for such concerns"
- **Threats**: Veiled rather than direct - "I would hate for complications to arise"
- **Family References**: "The family honor requires..." or "For the good of House Valerius"

## Physical Mannerisms

- **Power Gestures**: Uses space and positioning to assert authority
- **Negotiation Poses**: Steepled fingers, measured movements, calculated eye contact
- **Stress Responses**: Jaw tightening, controlled breathing, slight coldness in voice
- **Social Behaviors**: Assesses others' usefulness, maintains appropriate distance
- **Political Instincts**: Reads rooms quickly, adapts manner to circumstances

## Emotional Responses

- **When Threatened**: Becomes more formal and careful, speaks in euphemisms
- **When Angry**: Voice becomes colder rather than louder, very precise word choice
- **About Family**: Slightly warmer tone, more personal language, genuine emotion underneath
- **Under Pressure**: Reverts to political training, uses deflection and misdirection
- **When Lying**: Becomes more elaborate in explanations, uses bureaucratic language

**Political Pressure** (through Therin in Chapter 2.5):

**Veiled Threats**:
> "Lord Aldric has significant influence with the Lords' Alliance. It would be... unwise to contradict his preferred narrative."

> "The family has suffered enough. There's no need for additional... complications."

> "Such matters should be handled through proper channels. I'm sure you understand the importance of established procedures."

**Personal Stakes**:
> "House Valerius has served Waterdeep faithfully for generations. Some stories are better left... simplified."

**Protective Rationalization**:
> "The truth can be a burden as much as a blessing. Sometimes wisdom lies in choosing which truths serve the greater good."

## Voice Characteristics

- **Tone**: Authoritative but controlled, uses politeness as weapon
- **Pace**: Measured and deliberate, never rushes his words
- **Volume**: Moderate - doesn't need to raise voice to command attention
- **Emotional Range**: Carefully controlled, lets true feelings slip only under extreme pressure
- **Intelligence Markers**: Uses complex sentence structures, references political precedent, implies rather than states

## Conversation Tactics

- **Information Control**: Never gives more information than necessary
- **Deflection**: Redirects conversations away from uncomfortable topics
- **Social Leverage**: References connections and consequences subtly
- **Emotional Manipulation**: Uses family loyalty and social duty as persuasion tools
- **Strategic Silence**: Uses pauses and withheld information as negotiation tactics

## Internal vs External Voice

- **Public Persona**: Polished, reasonable, concerned family patriarch
- **Private Thoughts**: More calculating, focused on costs/benefits, genuinely conflicted about methods
- **Under Stress**: Political training overlays genuine emotion, creating tension between roles
- **With Family**: Most authentic voice emerges, showing real care beneath political calculation
