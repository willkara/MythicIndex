# Lord Aldric Valerius - Background

## Origin

- **Birthplace**: Waterdeep
- **Social Class**: Minor nobility, but politically ambitious
- **Cultural Background**: Traditional Waterdhavian aristocracy with strong emphasis on family reputation and political advancement

## Family

- **Parents**: Deceased House Valerius nobility who established the family's minor noble status
- **Siblings**: Unknown - likely the elder brother who inherited family leadership
- **Nephews**: 
  - Lysander Valerius (deceased) - disapproved of adventuring career
  - Cassius Valerius (living) - approves of scholarly pursuits at Great Library
- **Family Role**: Patriarch and political representative of House Valerius interests

## History

- **Early Career**: Rose through Waterdeep political circles by understanding power dynamics and building strategic relationships
- **Lords' Alliance Membership**: Gained influential position through careful networking and political maneuvering
- **Political Philosophy**: Family reputation and political stability matter more than individual truth or justice
- **The Cover-Up**: When Lysander died in the dragon cult attack, immediately moved to suppress true circumstances to prevent:
  - Political embarrassment to House Valerius
  - Potential investigation into family members' activities
  - Damage to Lords' Alliance reputation for protecting members

## Personal Journey

- **Core Motivation**: Protecting and advancing House Valerius political standing
- **Life Philosophy**: Public narratives must be controlled to maintain order and reputation
- **Greatest Fear**: Scandal or truth that could destroy family political influence
- **Moral Justification**: Believes covering up the dragon cult connection protects both family and public order
- **Current Challenge**: The Last Light Company's civic prominence and potential discovery of the truth

## Political Methods

- **Information Control**: Uses clerks and intermediaries to manage narratives
- **Financial Pressure**: Offers bribes and incentives to maintain silence
- **Political Leverage**: Threatens consequences for those who challenge his version of events
- **Relationship Management**: Maintains networks of favors and obligations
- **Crisis Management**: Swift action to contain potential scandals before they spread

## Character Function in Story

- **Political Antagonist**: Represents corrupt noble class that prioritizes reputation over truth
- **Family Tension**: Creates complex loyalties - he genuinely cares for family but in twisted way
- **Future Threat**: Potential enemy of Last Light Company if they threaten his constructed narratives
- **Moral Complexity**: Not pure evil - believes he's protecting family and preventing panic
- **Stakes Escalator**: His involvement makes every action politically dangerous for the Company

## Personal Details
- **Favorite Meal**: Veal medallions in saffron cream with truffled root vegetables. He hosts intimate dinners where the presentation is as calculated as any speech he delivers.
- **Favorite Drink**: A glass of amberfire cordial diluted with exactly two cubes of cut ice. He swirls it while weighing favors, claiming the ritual keeps his temper as controlled as his narratives.
