# Image Ideas for Lord Aldric Valerius

## Current Imagery
- **3 images** - Need 2+ more for minimum of 5

## Character Summary
Mid-50s patriarch of House Valerius and influential Lords' Alliance member. Father of deceased Lysander and living Cassius. Political antagonist who orchestrates cover-ups to protect family reputation. Silver-gray hair, sharp calculating eyes. Pragmatically ruthless - family reputation above truth or justice. Not the main villain but a political obstacle.

## Proposed Image Ideas

### 1. Power in the Shadows
**Scene:** Lord Aldric in private meeting, directing the cover-up of something that threatens House reputation.
- **Pose/Action:** Seated, documents before him, giving quiet but absolute instructions
- **Setting:** Private study, expensive but tasteful surroundings
- **Mood:** The machinery of political power, influence wielded quietly
- **Tags:** political, antagonist, scheming, powerful

### 2. Noble Patriarch
**Scene:** Official portrait of Lord Aldric in his role as Lords' Alliance member, projecting authority and respectability.

- **Mood:** The father who lost one son and shapes the narrative, complexity beneath villainy
- **Tags:** family, emotional-undertone, complex, private

### 4. Political Maneuvering
**Scene:** Lord Aldric at a Lords' Alliance function, navigating conversations and building influence.
- **Pose/Action:** Speaking with other nobles, commanding attention naturally
- **Setting:** Political gathering, noble attendees

- **Tags:** political, social, manipulative, aristocratic

### 5. Pragmatic Ruthlessness
**Scene:** Lord Aldric receiving unwelcome information and making the cold calculation to suppress it.
- **Pose/Action:** Reading a report, expression hardening, decision visible forming

- **Mood:** Family reputation above all, the cost of political survival
