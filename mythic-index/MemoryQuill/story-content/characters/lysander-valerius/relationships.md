# Lysander Valerius - Relationships

## Family Bonds
- **Cassius Valerius**: 
  - *Relationship Type*: Older brother, scholar at Great Library of Waterdeep
  - *History*: Cassius was always the bookish one while Lysander dreamed of adventure. Different paths but deep mutual respect and love.
  - *Current Status*: Deceased (Lysander died in Undershade Canyon) - Cassius unaware of true heroic circumstances
  - *Feelings Toward*: Deep affection, pride in Cassius's scholarly achievements, protective of his quieter brother
  - *Tensions/Issues*: Cassius worried Lysander's courage would get him killed (which ultimately proved prophetic)
  - *Shared Experiences*: Childhood in Waterdeep, regular visits where Lysander shared adventure stories with his scholarly brother

- **House Valerius (Parents)**:
  - *Relationship Type*: Noble parents of minor house
  - *History*: Raised both sons with noble education and expectations
  - *Current Status*: Unknown current status - received official notification of Lysander's death as "adventuring accident"
  - *Feelings Toward*: Traditional familial duty mixed with desire for independent heroic path
  - *Tensions/Issues*: Lysander chose adventuring over traditional noble pursuits
  - *Shared Experiences*: Noble upbringing, education in history and heroic traditions

- **Lord Aldric Valerius**:
  - *Relationship Type*: Uncle, influential noble with Lords' Alliance connections
  - *History*: Family patriarch who expected Lysander to follow traditional noble path rather than adventuring
  - *Current Status*: Deceased (Lysander died) - Aldric has been actively suppressing the true circumstances of death
  - *Feelings Toward*: Complex mix of familial obligation and resentment over Lysander's chosen path
  - *Tensions/Issues*: Aldric disapproved of adventuring career, now covering up dragon cult involvement to protect family reputation
  - *Shared Experiences*: Noble family gatherings, political expectations, Aldric's attempts to steer Lysander toward "respectable" pursuits
