# Image Ideas for Lysander Valerius

## Current Imagery
- **3 images** - Need 2+ more for minimum of 5

## Character Summary
Deceased human fighter who led the Gilded Compass and sacrificed himself at Undershade Canyon to save his companions. Son of Waterdhavian nobility (gilt rivets on his jack). His shield boss became part of Company heraldry. Final command to Veyra: "Live. Remember. Rescue." Represents the ultimate "no one left behind" failure that drives the Company's mission.

---

## Proposed Image Ideas

### 1. The Final Stand
**Scene:** Lysander using his shield to wedge open the closing escape route through molten glass, the moment of ultimate sacrifice.
- **Pose/Action:** Braced against impossible force, shield arm straining, face set with determination
- **Setting:** Collapsing canyon, molten glass flowing, escape visible beyond
- **Mood:** Sacrifice incarnate, the moment a legend is born
- **Tags:** climactic, sacrifice, dramatic, pivotal

### 2. "Live. Remember. Rescue."
**Scene:** The last look between Lysander and Veyra - his eyes carrying the command that would define her life.
- **Pose/Action:** Face in profile or three-quarter, eyes locked with unseen Veyra, lips forming the words
- **Setting:** Chaos fading to focus on that final gaze
- **Mood:** The weight of a final command, love expressed through duty
- **Tags:** emotional, pivotal-moment, two-character-implied, intense

### 3. Leader of the Gilded Compass
**Scene:** Lysander in better times - leading his party, the charismatic commander before tragedy.
- **Pose/Action:** At head of group, confident stride, hand raised directing, smile visible
- **Setting:** Road or pre-mission gathering, companions visible
- **Mood:** What was lost, the leader they followed willingly
- **Tags:** backstory, leadership, group-implied, contrast

### 4. The Shield Boss Legacy
**Scene:** Focus on Lysander's shield with its distinctive boss - the artifact that would become Company heraldry.
- **Pose/Action:** Lysander holding shield, boss catching light, or shield alone in dramatic lighting
- **Setting:** Variable - could be pre-battle or as recovered relic
- **Mood:** Symbol of sacrifice, the physical legacy
- **Tags:** artifact-focus, symbolic, heraldry-origin

### 5. Noble Son
**Scene:** Lysander in his element as minor nobility - the gilt rivets on his jack visible, comfortable bearing of someone raised to lead.
- **Pose/Action:** Standing in noble setting, perhaps at family estate or formal gathering
- **Setting:** Aristocratic surroundings, fine clothing
- **Mood:** The privilege that was set aside for adventure, duty over comfort
- **Tags:** backstory, nobility, contrast, character-origin

- Human male, approximately 25-30 at death
- Strong build capable of holding back molten glass
- Steady, bright eyes full of command
- Military-style jack with gilt rivets (sign of minor nobility)

- Measured tread, athletic and combat-trained
- Shield with distinctive boss (incorporated into Company heraldry)
- Expression: Steady, commanding, warm with his people
