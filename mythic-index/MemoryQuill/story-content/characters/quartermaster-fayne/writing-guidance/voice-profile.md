# Quartermaster Fayne - Voice Profile

**Character Role:** Pattern Analyst
**Core Voice:** Data-driven | Analytical precision | Pattern-focused

## Quantitative Profile

- **Average words/line:** 12.8
- **Contractions:** 18% (moderate)
- **Fragment usage:** 14%
- **Question ratio:** 16% (thoughtful inquiries)
- **Numerical references:** Contextual (specific when professionally relevant, qualitative in casual settings)

## Signature Phrases

- **"Pattern shows..."** / **"Pattern suggests..."** (Use when sharing professional analysis - ONLY Fayne uses these)
- **"Note for the record..."** (When documenting important information)
- **"Technically speaking..."** (When correcting inaccuracies)
- **Specific numbers when relevant** (In professional contexts where precision matters)
- **Can be vague in casual contexts** (Comfortable with "maybe," "probably," "I think" when precision isn't needed)

## Character-Specific Lexicon

**Professional/On-Duty:**
- "Pattern" - key analytical term when relevant
- Specific data when it supports the argument: "significant improvement," "multiple instances," "high probability"
- Analytical framework: "data suggests," "evidence indicates," "from a logistical standpoint"
- Efficient, objective language

**Personal/Off-Duty:**
- Simple, direct vocabulary
- Qualitative observations: "different," "changed," "seems like"
- Can use emotional language when appropriate: "I'm glad," "That's concerning," "I appreciate that"
- Natural conversational tone

## Voice Authenticity Scoring

Target 90+ for major scenes

**Vocabulary (25):** Contextually appropriate - analytical when on-duty, conversational when off-duty
**Syntax (25):** 11-15 words/line average, structured when working, relaxed in casual settings
**Rhythm (25):** Measured and thoughtful, varies by context
**Content (25):** Reflects Fayne's three core identities - Transformer, Archivist, Logistician - not just analysis
