# Image Ideas for Cassius Valerius

## Current Imagery
- **3 images** - Need 2+ more for minimum of 5

## Character Summary
Scholar and brother of the deceased Lysander Valerius. Rescued from the Great Library collapse by the Last Light Company. Bears strong family resemblance to Lysander in jawline and steady gaze. His rescue provides Veyra a measure of redemption for failing to save his brother. Late 20s/early 30s, academic robes, calm even when injured.

---

## Proposed Image Ideas

### 1. Trapped in the Archives
**Scene:** Cassius in the collapsed sub-archives, injured but composed, waiting for rescue.
- **Pose/Action:** Seated against rubble, leg at wrong angle (compound fracture), expression calm and determined
- **Setting:** Collapsed library section, dust and debris, faint light
- **Mood:** Courage in adversity, the scholar's discipline under pressure
- **Tags:** rescue-scene, trapped, dramatic, determined

### 2. Family Resemblance
**Scene:** Portrait highlighting Cassius's features that echo his brother Lysander - the jawline, the steady gaze, the ghost of the lost.
- **Pose/Action:** Direct gaze, scholarly pose, Lysander's echo visible
- **Setting:** Academic setting or neutral background
- **Mood:** The living reminder, loss and continuity
- **Tags:** portrait, family, symbolic, emotional

### 3. Meeting Veyra
**Scene:** The moment Cassius sees Veyra and recognizes something - or Veyra sees the brother of the man she couldn't save.
- **Pose/Action:** Eyes meeting, understanding passing, the weight of history
- **Setting:** Rescue aftermath, medical attention happening
- **Mood:** Redemption offered, the past connecting to the present
- **Tags:** two-character, emotional, pivotal, meaningful

### 4. The Scholar's Work
**Scene:** Cassius in his element - surrounded by books and manuscripts in the Great Library, before the collapse.
- **Pose/Action:** Reading or writing, absorbed in scholarly pursuit
- **Setting:** Library sub-archives, shelves and scrolls surrounding
- **Mood:** The life of the mind, purpose before disaster
- **Tags:** backstory, scholarly, atmospheric, peaceful

### 5. Healing and Gratitude
**Scene:** Cassius after Aldwin heals his compound fracture, expressing gratitude with the composure of someone who understands what was almost lost.
- **Pose/Action:** Testing healed leg, looking at Company members with genuine thankfulness
- **Setting:** Medical area, post-rescue
- **Mood:** Lives saved, the purpose fulfilled, debt acknowledged
- **Tags:** aftermath, emotional, healing, grateful
