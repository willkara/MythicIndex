## Notes
*   **From Stablehand to Soldier**: Started as a simple animal caretaker but proved his mettle during the Battle for Westwall, transitioning from a nervous young man into a competent and respected combatant.
*   **Tactical Archer**: Demonstrated exceptional, self-taught skill with a bow, making tactically brilliant shots under pressure that saved lives and controlled the flow of battle, earning the public praise of veterans like Archer Venn.
*   **Bond Forged in Fire**: Was saved from certain death by Vera's dire wolf transformation. He repaid this life-debt moments later by being the first to stand and form a protective circle around her when she collapsed, showcasing immense courage and solidifying a deep, loyal bond between them.
*   **Proven Courage**: Faced down one of Krag's elite mercenaries and, later, stood with the last defenders against overwhelming odds, overcoming his initial fear to prove his loyalty to his found family.
*   **Dual Role**: Continues to maintain his responsibilities for animal care while now being recognized as a reliable archer and member of the Company's defensive force.
