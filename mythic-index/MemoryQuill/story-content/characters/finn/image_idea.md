# Image Ideas for Finn

## Current Imagery
- **3 images** - Need 2+ more for minimum of 5

## Character Summary
Young human fighter who evolved from nervous stablehand to competent Company archer. Saved from certain death by Vera's dire wolf transformation during the Battle of Westwall, then immediately stood to protect her when she collapsed. Natural talent for tactical archery. Represents the emerging hero archetype - courage found through crisis.

---

## Proposed Image Ideas

### 1. Facing Death - Serka's Shadow
**Scene:** The pivotal moment - Finn on the ground, wounded, looking up at Serka's raised axes, seeing his death in the polished steel.
- **Pose/Action:** On his back, knife shattered, hand raised defensively, terror and defiance in his eyes
- **Setting:** Westwall courtyard, chaos around, Serka's shadow falling over him
- **Mood:** The moment before salvation, terror and courage intertwined
- **Tags:** pivotal-moment, dramatic, vulnerable, action

### 2. Standing Vigil for Vera
**Scene:** Finn forming the protective circle around collapsed Vera, the first to stand when others hesitated.
- **Pose/Action:** Standing over her unconscious form, facing outward, arms spread protectively
- **Setting:** Aftermath of battle, smoke clearing, other Company members joining
- **Mood:** Courage repaying a life-debt, found family protecting its own
- **Tags:** protective, emotional, foundational-moment, group

### 3. The Tactical Shot
**Scene:** Finn making one of his brilliant battlefield shots - threading an arrow between allies to disable an enemy threat.
- **Pose/Action:** Full draw, intense concentration, eye along arrow shaft
- **Setting:** Battle chaos, allies visible in foreground, target beyond
- **Mood:** Natural talent manifesting, the moment a boy becomes a soldier
- **Tags:** archery, action, precision, skill

### 4. Stablehand's Comfort
**Scene:** Finn in his element - caring for horses in the stable, the gentle side of the warrior.
- **Pose/Action:** Brushing a horse, speaking softly, at ease with animals
- **Setting:** Stable interior, warm light, hay and tack
- **Mood:** The person behind the fighter, tenderness and competence
- **Tags:** civilian, animals, warm, character-depth

### 5. Learning from Venn
**Scene:** Finn receiving instruction from Archer Venn, the veteran passing wisdom to the natural talent.
- **Pose/Action:** Both examining arrows or bow technique, student and mentor dynamic
- **Setting:** Training area or workshop
- **Mood:** Knowledge passed down, the next generation rising
- **Tags:** two-character, mentorship, training, warm

### 6. Walking Beside Vera's Wagon
**Scene:** During the road home, Finn walking alongside Vera's wagon, hand on the wood, keeping vigil over his savior.
- **Pose/Action:** Walking, bandaged ribs visible, hand touching wagon, looking at unconscious Vera
- **Setting:** Road south, cavalry escort around, somber procession
- **Mood:** Vigil, gratitude, the bond forged in blood
- **Tags:** emotional, journey, devotion, quiet
