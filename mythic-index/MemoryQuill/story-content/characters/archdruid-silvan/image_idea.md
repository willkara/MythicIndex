# Image Ideas for Archdruid Silvan Oakshield

## Current Imagery
- **1 image:** Full-body forest portrait (oil-style, dark-fantasy) - standing with gnarled living staff

## Character Summary
Ancient elven Archdruid and Circle Leader who arrives to help Vera recover from her dire wolf transformation trauma. Represents druidic community, wisdom, and the concept of found family. Can transform into a golden eagle.

---

## Proposed Image Ideas

### 1. Golden Eagle Transformation
**Scene:** Silvan in mid-transformation or fully transformed as a majestic golden eagle soaring above the Thornwood canopy.
- **Pose/Action:** Wings spread wide, caught between forms OR fully eagle with wise, knowing eyes
- **Setting:** Open sky above ancient forest at dawn/dusk
- **Mood:** Freedom, ancient power, the duality of druidic nature
- **Tags:** transformation, flight, golden-eagle, mystical, action

### 2. Healing Ritual at Westwall
**Scene:** Silvan kneeling beside unconscious Vera, hands emanating soft green druidic energy as he works to stabilize her transformation trauma.
- **Pose/Action:** Hands hovering over Vera's form, eyes closed in concentration, staff planted beside him glowing faintly
- **Setting:** Makeshift infirmary tent, warm candlelight mixed with ethereal green glow
- **Mood:** Compassion, gentle power, sanctuary
- **Tags:** healing, magic, druidic-ritual, tender-moment, two-character

### 3. Circle of Druids Arrival
**Scene:** Silvan leading his delegation of druids as they approach the damaged Westwall Watchpost, determination in his stride.
- **Pose/Action:** Walking at the head of a small group, staff in hand, silver hair catching wind
- **Setting:** Road approaching Westwall at twilight, damaged walls visible ahead
- **Mood:** Purpose, hope arriving, community responding to crisis
- **Tags:** group-shot, arrival, leadership, dramatic-lighting

### 4. Ancient Grove Communion
**Scene:** Silvan standing in a sacred grove, both hands pressed against an enormous ancient tree, communing with the forest's spirit.
- **Pose/Action:** Eyes closed, forehead nearly touching bark, staff leaning nearby, surrounded by faint nature spirits or motes of light
- **Setting:** Deep forest clearing, shafts of light filtering through canopy, moss-covered stones
- **Mood:** Reverence, connection, timeless wisdom
- **Tags:** mystical, communion, forest, meditation, magical

### 5. Staff of Living Wood
**Scene:** Close-up study of Silvan channeling power through his living staff, fresh leaves sprouting and glowing with inner light.
- **Pose/Action:** Both hands gripping staff, magic visibly flowing upward through the spiraling wood
- **Setting:** Darker background to emphasize the magical glow
- **Mood:** Power contained and controlled, natural magic made visible
- **Tags:** magic-focus, close-up, artifact, druidic-power

### 6. "One of Ours"
**Scene:** The moment Silvan first sees Vera and recognizes her as family despite her being self-taught - a look of immediate acceptance and concern.
- **Pose/Action:** Standing in doorway of infirmary, hand raised slightly as if to bless or comfort, expression soft yet serious
- **Setting:** Threshold between outdoor light and interior shadow
- **Mood:** Recognition, acceptance, quiet emotion
- **Tags:** emotional-moment, portrait, doorway-framing

---

## Visual Consistency Notes
- Silver-white hair should flow past shoulders with full thick beard
- Deep-set, shadowed eyes that convey ancient wisdom
- Robes in moss-green and muddy browns, layered and organic
- Staff always shows signs of living growth (leaves, spiraling wood)
- Pointed elven ears visible through hair
- Weathered but dignified bearing
