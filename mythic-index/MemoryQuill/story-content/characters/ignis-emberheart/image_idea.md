# Image Ideas for Ignis "Spark" Emberheart

## Current Imagery
- **3 images** - Need 2+ more for minimum of 5

## Character Summary
Fire genasi sorcerer whose wild magic surge catastrophically amplified the cult's ritual at Undershade Canyon. Eyes that "shone" with fascination, flamboyant and dramatic. Last seen alive but trapped behind molten glass, fate unresolved. His desperate plea "Scout, find another path—live—" haunts Veyra. Represents the unfinished business of "no one left behind." **Status: Missing (possible transformation/altered state)**

## Proposed Image Ideas

### 1. The Catastrophic Surge
**Scene:** Ignis at ground-zero of the wild magic catastrophe, flames wreathing him like a "crown of regret" as his magic feeds rather than disrupts the cult's ritual.
- **Pose/Action:** Arms outstretched, horror on face as power spirals beyond control
- **Setting:** Cult ritual site, magical energies cascading wrongly
- **Mood:** Tragedy of good intentions, power beyond mastery
- **Tags:** climactic, magic, disaster, pivotal

### 2. Flames Like a Crown
**Scene:** The haunting image of Ignis standing in the chaos, fire wreathing his head like a crown made of regret and desperate hope.
- **Pose/Action:** Standing tall amid destruction, flames forming crown-like pattern around head
- **Setting:** Collapsing canyon, light from flames and magic
- **Mood:** Beauty in tragedy, the iconic image of failure
- **Tags:** symbolic, dramatic, iconic, magical

### 3. Desperate Counter
**Scene:** Ignis attempting to undo what he caused, pushing magic outward in futile hope, determination in every line of his body.
- **Pose/Action:** Hands thrust forward, body straining, face set with desperate determination
- **Setting:** Magical chaos, ritual effects visible
- **Mood:** The hero's attempt that came too late
- **Tags:** action, magic, desperate, heroic-failure

### 4. Sealed Behind Glass
**Scene:** The last glimpse - Ignis visible through rapidly solidifying molten glass, trapped but possibly alive.
- **Pose/Action:** Hand pressed against the barrier, mouth open in final words, figure distorting
- **Setting:** Glass flowing and hardening, world beyond becoming unreachable
- **Mood:** The unresolved mystery, hope or horror frozen in amber
- **Tags:** haunting, mysterious, final-moment, unresolved

### 5. Before the Fall
**Scene:** Ignis in happier times with the Gilded Compass - the flamboyant sorcerer entertaining with casual displays of fire magic.
- **Pose/Action:** Dramatic gesture, flames dancing from fingers, delighted smile
- **Setting:** Camp or tavern, companions watching with varying amusement
- **Mood:** What was lost, the joy before tragedy
- **Tags:** backstory, contrast, magical, joyful
