  - *History*: Their professional relationship continues at the Westwall Watchpost, where Thorne relies on Sarge's discipline.
  - *Current Status*: Professional colleagues.
  - *Professional Opinion of*: Values his discipline and practical approach.

- **Grimjaw Ironbeard**: 
  - *Nature of Relationship*: Fellow Watchpost veteran, heavy rescue specialist.
  - *Hierarchy*: Peers in experience, but different roles.
  - *Dynamics*: Shares a gruff, professional respect, with Grimjaw often challenging Sarge's by-the-book approach with practical wisdom. They often interact in the training yard.

  - *Current Status*: Professional colleagues with a gruff dynamic.

  - *Professional Opinion of*: Grudging respect for their effectiveness, despite their unconventional methods.
