# Image Ideas for Thorin Ironbark

## Current Imagery
- **4 images** - Need 1+ more for minimum of 5

## Character Summary
Elderly dwarf druid with moss-intertwined beard. Represents the unusual combination of dwarven heritage and druidic calling. His beard literally growing with living plants shows decades of connection to earth and growth. The stone-speaker who learned to hear the green.

---

## Proposed Image Ideas

### 1. The Living Beard
**Scene:** Close study of Thorin's distinctive beard with moss and small plants actually growing within it - nature's endorsement of his calling.
- **Pose/Action:** Face portrait, beard prominent, tiny flowers or moss visibly alive
- **Setting:** Natural lighting to show growth is real
- **Mood:** Nature embracing the unlikely druid, dedication made visible
- **Tags:** portrait, distinctive-feature, druidic, unique

### 2. Stone Meets Green
**Scene:** Thorin demonstrating his unusual specialty - speaking to stone with druidic magic, bridging dwarven and natural worlds.
- **Pose/Action:** Hand on rock face, eyes closed, communing with both earth and growth
- **Setting:** Cave entrance or cliff face with plants
- **Mood:** The bridge between traditions, unique calling fulfilled
- **Tags:** magic, communion, unique, druidic

### 3. Teaching Younger Druids
**Scene:** Thorin sharing his unusual perspective with other druids - the wisdom that comes from an unexpected path.
- **Pose/Action:** Seated with younger druids, sharing stories or demonstrating technique
- **Setting:** Natural grove or druid circle
- **Mood:** Wisdom from unconventional sources, tradition expanded
- **Tags:** teaching, group-scene, wisdom, mentorship

### 4. Dwarven Druid's Prayer
**Scene:** Thorin in druidic meditation or prayer, the unlikely combination of dwarven practicality and natural mysticism.
- **Pose/Action:** Kneeling or sitting, communion posture, peaceful expression
- **Setting:** Sacred natural space
- **Mood:** Faith found in unexpected places, the peaceful elder
- **Tags:** meditation, spiritual, peaceful, druidic

### 5. Growth from Stone
**Scene:** Thorin using his magic to encourage plants to grow from seemingly barren rock - his signature ability.
- **Pose/Action:** Hands extended toward stone, green shoots emerging, magic visible
- **Setting:** Rocky terrain transforming under his touch
- **Mood:** Life finding purchase, the impossible made natural
- **Tags:** magic, transformation, growth, powerful
