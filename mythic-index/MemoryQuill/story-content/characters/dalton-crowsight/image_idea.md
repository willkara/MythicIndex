# Image Ideas for Sergeant Dalton Crowsight

## Current Imagery
- **3 images** - Need 2+ more for minimum of 5

## Character Summary
Veridian Agronomics security officer and Silent Hand operative. Mid-30s with athletic predatory build. Professional interrogator who tests people with fabricated information. Enjoys psychological warfare. Was spectacularly outmaneuvered by Lyra Kestrel at the gala - a rare defeat that unsettled him. Works alongside Captain Thale Greycloak.

---

## Proposed Image Ideas

### 1. The Fabricated Trap
**Scene:** Crowsight setting his signature trap - casually mentioning fabricated information to catch someone in a lie.
- **Pose/Action:** Leaning in slightly, wine glass in hand, studying reaction
- **Setting:** Gala or formal setting
- **Mood:** The predator's patience, psychological warfare in polite conversation
- **Tags:** antagonist, social, calculating, subtle

### 2. Outmaneuvered by Lyra
**Scene:** The moment Crowsight realizes Lyra has beaten him at his own game - rare confusion replacing confidence.
- **Pose/Action:** Slight backward lean, brow furrowed, mask of composure cracking
- **Setting:** Gala, Lyra visible in confident pose opposite
- **Mood:** The hunter become hunted, professional pride wounded
- **Tags:** defeat, two-character, antagonist, pivotal

### 3. Predatory Assessment
**Scene:** Crowsight watching guests arrive at a Veridian event, cataloguing threats with cold calculation.
- **Pose/Action:** Standing apart, eyes tracking movement, slight smile of anticipation
- **Setting:** Gala entrance or security post
- **Mood:** The hunter among sheep, identifying weaknesses
- **Tags:** surveillance, antagonist, calculating, atmospheric

### 4. Interrogator's Tools
**Scene:** Crowsight in a more private setting, the interrogator's true nature visible without social masks.
- **Pose/Action:** Seated or standing in dominant position, hands relaxed, eyes sharp
- **Setting:** Office or interrogation-appropriate room
- **Mood:** The cruelty he enjoys, professional at inflicting pressure
- **Tags:** intimate, threatening, antagonist, dark

### 5. Working with Greycloak
**Scene:** Crowsight and Captain Greycloak conferring on security threats - the counter-intelligence mind meeting the tactical enforcer.
- **Pose/Action:** Heads together over documents or surveillance, professional collaboration
- **Setting:** Security office at Veridian
- **Mood:** Evil competence, the apparatus of oppression
- **Tags:** two-character, antagonist, professional, tactical
