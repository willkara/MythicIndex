# Image Ideas for Corporal Hayes

## Current Imagery
- **3 images** - Need 2+ more for minimum of 5

## Character Summary
City Watch scout who returned wounded with critical intelligence about the Black Hawks force. Arrow wound in shoulder but insisted on fighting anyway, using crossbow with his good arm. Represents the determination of the Watch scouts who discovered the enemy and paid the price. Refuses to quit despite wounds.

---

## Proposed Image Ideas

### 1. Wounded Return
**Scene:** Hayes and other scouts riding through Westwall gates, wounded but bearing critical intelligence.
- **Pose/Action:** Slumped in saddle, arrow still in shoulder, but eyes alert with urgent information
- **Setting:** Westwall gates, defenders rushing to meet them
- **Mood:** Mission accomplished at cost, the messenger's duty fulfilled
- **Tags:** arrival, wounded, dramatic, urgent

### 2. One Good Arm
**Scene:** Hayes in the battle, crossbow braced against his wounded side, firing with his good arm, refusing to be sidelined.
- **Pose/Action:** Awkward but effective shooting position, face set with determination
- **Setting:** Westwall battlements during the fight
- **Mood:** Courage beyond capability, the will that won't surrender
- **Tags:** combat, wounded, determined, action

### 3. Intelligence Briefing
**Scene:** Hayes delivering his report about the Black Hawks camp despite his wounds, duty first.
- **Pose/Action:** Standing or seated with medic working on him, talking through gritted teeth
- **Setting:** Watchpost interior, officers listening
- **Mood:** Information more important than pain, professional to the core
- **Tags:** briefing, wounded, professional, determined

### 4. Scout in the Field
**Scene:** Hayes before the wounds - silent observation of enemy camp, professional reconnaissance.
- **Pose/Action:** Prone or concealed, counting enemy forces, making mental notes
- **Setting:** Forest overlooking Black Hawks camp
- **Mood:** The dangerous work of seeing without being seen
- **Tags:** backstory, scouting, stealth, professional

### 5. Arrow Wound Treatment
**Scene:** Hayes having the arrow removed or wound treated, stoic endurance visible.
- **Pose/Action:** Braced for pain, jaw clenched, eyes focused elsewhere
- **Setting:** Medical area, healer working
- **Mood:** Pain accepted as cost of duty, soldier's endurance
- **Tags:** medical, wounded, stoic, intimate

- Expression: Determined, refuses to quit
- Body language: Compensating for wound but pressing forward
