# Image Ideas for Elara Whisperwind

## Current Imagery
- **4 images** - Need 1+ more for minimum of 5

## Character Summary
Young adult elf rogue with pointed ears and dark hooded cloak. Specializes in stealth and infiltration work for the Company. Silent, observant, able to go where others cannot. The shadow who gathers intelligence and opens paths.

---

## Proposed Image Ideas

### 1. Shadow Work
**Scene:** Elara blending into darkness, only a hint of her presence visible - the rogue at her best.
- **Pose/Action:** Moving through shadows, barely visible, eyes catching faint light
- **Setting:** Dark corridor or night scene
- **Mood:** The unseen observer, stealth made art
- **Tags:** stealth, atmospheric, mysterious, skilled

### 2. Opening the Way
**Scene:** Elara at work on a lock or mechanism, expert hands finding the path.
- **Pose/Action:** Picking lock or disabling trap, tools in precise motion
- **Setting:** Door, chest, or mechanism close-up
- **Mood:** Every lock has a key if you know how to ask
- **Tags:** skill, lockpicking, close-up, professional

### 3. Elven Grace in Darkness
**Scene:** Portrait showing Elara's elven features beneath the dark hood - pointed ears, keen eyes, youthful but serious.
- **Pose/Action:** Face partially shadowed by hood, one ear visible, direct gaze
- **Setting:** Low light, dramatic shadows
- **Mood:** Youth and danger combined, the next generation of shadows
- **Tags:** portrait, shadowed, mysterious, elven

### 4. Reporting Back
**Scene:** Elara returning from reconnaissance, sharing intelligence with the Company, stepping from shadow to light.
- **Pose/Action:** Emerging from darkness, speaking quietly to Veyra or Thorne
- **Setting:** Company meeting area, transition between dark and light
- **Mood:** The shadow's gift - knowledge bought with silence
- **Tags:** intelligence, two-character, transition, professional

### 5. Above the Action
**Scene:** Elara in an elevated position - rooftop, rafters, tree - observing a scene below.
- **Pose/Action:** Crouched or lying flat, looking down, recording information
- **Setting:** High vantage point over target area
- **Mood:** The watcher unseen, information flowing upward
- **Tags:** surveillance, elevated, observant, stealthy
