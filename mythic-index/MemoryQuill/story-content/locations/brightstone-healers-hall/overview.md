# Brightstone Healers' Hall – Overview

## Basic Information
- **Type**: Advanced medical facility combining magical and mundane healing arts
- **Region**: Waterdeep, within walking distance of Westwall Watchpost
- **Coordinates/Position**: Central Waterdeep, easily accessible from Watch facilities
- **First Appearance**: Chapter 2, Scene 1 (Waking in White-Fire Dreams)
- **Significance Level**: Critical - Site of Veyra's physical and philosophical recovery

## Quick Description
An urban civic-medical hall of pale ashlar with tall mullioned windows and gauze screens, organized for hygiene-first workflows: ground-floor triage and operating theatres under shadowless rune-lamp grids, a mezzanine sterilization/linen/apothecary band, quiet recovery and isolation wards above, and attic vent cowls with a drying loft over cellar boilers, cisterns, and waste staging. The air carries thyme and antiseptic.


## Visual Summary
- Pale limestone/white granite ashlar; broad mullioned windows with gauze curtains
- Ground: reception and triage, 2–3 operating theatres with scrub anterooms, prep/recovery niches
- Mezzanine: sterilization suite (copper boilers, instrument baths), linen rooms, apothecary and stillroom
- Upper: recovery wards, isolation stack with segregated flue, day room; nurse stations every two bays
- Attic/Roof: vent cowls, drying loft with slatted floors; soot filters and skylight shutters
- Cellar: boilers and covered cisterns, herb stills, sealed infectious waste staging
- Systems: passive stack ventilation, dwarven rune-lamp lighting grids, stone drains with scrub grates and limewash finishes
