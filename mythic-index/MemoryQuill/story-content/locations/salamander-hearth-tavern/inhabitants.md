## Space Usage Mapping

### The Hearth (Central Feature)
- **Use**: Focal point of establishment; warmth and light source; symbolic gathering center
- **Behavior**: Conversations orient toward the hearth; toasts raised in its light
- **Notes**: The hearth is the establishment's identity—"Salamander's Hearth" [FIRM ch2.5]

### Main Floor / Common Room
- **Use**: General seating; storytelling; communal discussions
- **Population**: Mixed working-class groups; reverent atmosphere when discussing recent events
- **Behavior**: Conversation flows freely; debates emerge organically; collective toasts [FIRM ch2.5]

### Corner Stage/Alcove
- **Use**: Live music from moon-elf fiddlers [FIRM ch2.5]
- **Notes**: Creates ambient backdrop; music continues through discussions

### Ceiling Beams
- **Feature**: Low blackened beams painted amber by oil-lantern light [FIRM ch2.5]
- **Notes**: "Like honey on old wood"—defines the visual character

- **Tone**: Reverent but not solemn; awe mixed with practical appreciation [FIRM ch2.5]

- **Collective Actions**: Group toasts; prayers for fallen souls [FIRM ch2.5]

## Crowd Behavior Patterns
- **Entry of News**: When significant events occur, patrons share and process collectively
- **Debate Protocol**: Skeptics like Gillthorn voice counterpoints; others respond with witness accounts
- **Resolution**: Despite debate, underlying current of appreciation and respect [FIRM ch2.5]
