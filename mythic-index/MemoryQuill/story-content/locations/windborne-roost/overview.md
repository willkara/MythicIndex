# The Windborne Roost – Overview

## Basic Information
- **Type**: Roadside Inn/Tavern with City Watch Connections
- **Region**: North Road, crossroads waystation between Waterdeep and northern settlements
- **Position**: Strategic crossroads offering commanding views of surrounding countryside
- **First Appearance**: Chapter 28 - The Road North
- **Significance Level**: Recurring Location (Safe House)

## Quick Description
A hilltop inn-and-trade compound arranged around a cobbled courtyard: a heavy timber longhouse tavern with twin chimney stacks, a northeast guest wing, a west stable and coach yard, and a southeast market arcade. Low fieldstone walls, crowned yard drains, and a discrete roof perch with commanding sight lines make it an ideal northern waystation with built-in observation and weather resilience.

## Visual Summary
- Heavy timber frames on fieldstone plinths; cedar-shingle roofs with snow guards and copper-capped chimneys
- Central cobbled courtyard, crowned with trench drains to silt box; lamp posts and bell frame at gate
- Main Tavern: longhouse common room, corner stage, twin hearths; rear service doors to market and yard
- Guest Wing: 1.5-storey rooms under dormers; interior plaster; insulated with straw/clay infill
- Stables: center-aisle plan, 12–16 stalls, hayloft with trolley, drainage to manure pit and reed bed
- Market Arcade: shuttered post-and-beam bays with fold-down awnings; two discreet service hatches to tavern
- Observation Perch: cupola platform above tavern gable with sight lines down both approach roads

## Story Role
- **Plot Relevance**: Strategic waypoint for reconnaissance missions, intelligence gathering hub
- **Symbolic Meaning**: Bridge between official authority and field operations, safety before danger
- **Recurring Location**: Yes - serves as waystation for northern operations
- **Cover Operations**: Provides legitimate cover for government missions under agricultural/merchant facades

## Operational Details
- **Proprietor**: Quierk (aarakocra with keen observational skills)
- **Cover Story**: Legitimate agricultural supply hub serving farming communities
- **Authorization**: Silver City Watch token required for official services
- **Security Level**: Moderate - maintains genuine civilian traffic while providing official support
- **Intelligence Value**: Excellent position for monitoring northern road traffic and unusual activity
