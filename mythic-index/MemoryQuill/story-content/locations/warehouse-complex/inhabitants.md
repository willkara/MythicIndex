# Dock Ward Warehouse Complex – Inhabitants

Note: Minimal social detail. This file maps who occupies which physical spaces and when. The complex has no public residential population; occupancy is operational and invitation-only.

## Basic Demographics
- Regular Occupancy: 20–30 Last Light Company members and support staff
- Visitors: By appointment/escort only (officials, contractors, vendors)
- Oversight: Internal command; City Watch coordination as required
- Languages in Use: Common predominates; others ad hoc

## Space Usage (By Area)

### Perimeter Wall, Gatehouse, and Frontage Lane
- Gate Post: Queue ropes, guest screening, duty board, alarm bell
- Typical Occupants: Gate detail (rotating), messenger intake
- Notes: Gate secured at night; wicket for foot traffic only

### Central Courtyard (Cobbled Yard)
- Uses: Drill and training lanes, cart turn radius, staging/lay-down
- Typical Occupants: Training teams, porters, riggers
- Notes: Bucket/sand ranks and hand-pump access along walls

### Covered Walkways (Between Buildings A/B/C)
- Uses: Weather-protected circulation; fire doors at intervals
- Typical Occupants: Staff transiting during foul weather or drills
- Notes: Lantern brackets at bay intervals

### Building A — Main Warehouse
- Ground Floor
  - Zones: Briefing Circle (map table), The Mess (kitchen/service wall), Loading-bay training strip
  - Typical Occupants: All-hands briefing; meal service; training squads
- Mezzanine (Perimeter Quarters/Offices)
  - Quarters/Offices: Veyra Thornwake (Cmdr), Thorne Brightward (Deputy), Marcus Heartbridge (Intel), Aldwin Gentleheart, Grimjaw Ironbeard, Vera Moonwhisper, Sgt. Kelen, Cpl. Darric, Archer Venn, Medic Halden, Quartermaster Fayne, Finn, Mistress Elba, guest cubicles
  - Typical Occupants: Residents during off-duty; planning/admin during day
- Notes: Duty rosters posted near stairs; mezzanine baffles for noise control

### Building B — Workshop (Forge & Engineering)
- Zones: Central forge hearth, general benches, tool racks, materials storage, Cid’s drafting and engineering tables (leeward side)
- Typical Occupants: Grimjaw (lead), porters/assistants; Cidrella “Cid” Vexweld (frequent)
- Notes: Jib rails/chain hoist overhead; ventilation cowls and duct network in service

### Building C — Infirmary (Treatment & Recovery)
- Zones: Treatment Rooms (2), Recovery Ward (6–10 beds), Isolation Cubicles, Aldwin’s circular herb-lined Sanctuary
- Typical Occupants: Aldwin Gentleheart (lead), Medic Halden; patients as needed
- Notes: Quiet wing; controlled airflow and low-noise lighting

### Service Alley (East)
- Uses: Waste removal, vendor deliveries, night soil pickup
- Typical Occupants: Porters, vetted vendors under escort
- Notes: Not for guest access; stretcher clearance to gate maintained

## Occupancy Rhythms
- Pre-Dawn: Forge rekindle; Veyra’s lantern ritual; kitchen prep; gate opens for deliveries
- Day: Briefings and drills in A/yard; fabrication in B; treatment/rounds in C
- Evening: Shared meal in A; maintenance and planning; reduced shop activity
- Night: Gate secured; watch rotations; lamp-lit mezzanine; quiet wing in C

## Access Permissions
- Guests/Visitors: Gate screening; escorted; no workshop/infirmary access without approval
- Staff/Residents: Full access per duty; undercroft/mechanical only by authorization
- City Watch: Coordination as mission requires; no standing post within compound
