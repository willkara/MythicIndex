## Intelligence Value

### What Flows Through
- Information on magical artifacts
- Market intelligence on arcane goods
- Names and connections (within limits)
- Warning systems (who's asking, who's protected)

### What's Off-Limits
- Certain protected individuals (Korrath, etc.)
- Topics that draw unwanted attention
- Information that could expose networks

## Cultural Significance
The Silken Siren represents Waterdeep's shadow economy—where information is currency and discretion is survival. Unlike public taverns where news flows freely, here every word is weighed. It's a place where the wrong question can be fatal, and where even professional fences know when to refuse gold.
