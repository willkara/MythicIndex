# Azure Pavilion – Overview

## Basic Information
- **Type**: Civic/Entertainment Venue
- **Region**: Waterdeep Harbor District  
- **Coordinates/Position**: Waterfront pier overlooking Waterdeep Harbor
- **First Appearance**: Chapter 19 - An Olive Branch
- **Significance Level**: Important

## Quick Description
An open-air marble colonnade set on a pile-supported pier, crowned by a broad azure-glazed tile canopy and ringed by a low balustrade above the water. Enchanted crystal lanterns and deep eaves create a luminous, weather-sheltered arcade with uninterrupted harbor views and a controlled single approach.

## Visual Summary  
- White marble floor on granite stringers over oak piles; fluted column grid on ~20 ft bays  
- Azure-glazed multi-gable roof with copper ridge, gutters, and canvas storm-curtain rigging  
- Perimeter marble balustrade; pierhead gate with removable chain barriers  
- Capacity ~400–600 standing; service float/hoist on leeward side; lantern ring points throughout

## Story Role
- **Plot Relevance**: 
  - *Main Story*: Location of Captain Grimsby's "Civic Appreciation Gathering" that formally recognized the Last Light Company
  - *Subplots*: Established Company's legitimacy within Waterdeep's political structure
- **Symbolic Meaning**: Represents the Company's integration into Waterdeep's political and social framework
- **Recurring Location**: Potentially - established as a major civic venue
- **Key Events Occurring Here**: Last Light Company's formal civic recognition ceremony

## Key Features
- **Notable Landmarks**: Azure-glazed canopy, marble balustrade, pierhead gate, enchanted crystal lantern arrays, panoramic harbor vista
- **Unique Aspects**: Pile-supported open-air marble pavilion with fluted columns; engineered scuppers/drainage, windbreak curtains, and lightning finial
- **Hazards/Dangers**: High winds and salt spray creating slick surfaces; storm surge/king tides; mitigated by balustrades, chokepoint access, and City Watch oversight
- **Resources/Attractions**: High-quality catering, elegant atmosphere, networking opportunities with city elite

## Connections
- **Connected Locations**:
  - *Waterdeep Harbor*: Direct waterfront access
  - *Dock Ward*: Located within district boundaries
- **Transportation Methods**: Walk across the pier from the harbor district; controlled service-float/boat access on the leeward side for deliveries and maintenance
- **Distance from Other Key Locations**: Harbor district of Waterdeep

## Accessibility
- **Who Can Access**: Invitation-only events, city officials, distinguished guests
- **Restrictions**: Formal attire typically required, security screening for major events
- **Hidden Aspects**: None known
- **Secret Paths/Entrances**: None; side ladder to service float is maintenance-only and access-controlled

## Map References
- **World Map Position**: Waterdeep harbor waterfront
