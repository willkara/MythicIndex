# Stonebridge – Overview

## Basic Information

- **Type**: Market Town and River Crossing
- **Region**: The Sword Coast, along the Trade Way
- **Coordinates/Position**: A day's ride from The Wayward Compass inn
- **First Appearance**: Chapter 11 (Arrival in Stonebridge)
- **Significance Level**: Moderate

## Quick Description
A river-straddling trade hub defined by an ancient five-arch stone bridge over the Silver Rush, a paved market square with guild arcades and a central fountain, and a busy west-bank quay lined with warehouses and cranes. The town’s civic stonework and timber dockworks show centuries of maintenance against flood, ice, and the constant wear of commerce.

## Visual Summary
- **Style & Materials**: Ashlar bridge piers and arches with parapets; granite/basalt cobbles at the market; dressed-stone guild façades with slate roofs; timber wharf piles, tarred planks, iron bollards and capstans.
- **Site & Layout**: Five-arch bridge as central spine; east-bank market square, fountain, guild hall row, Shepherd’s Rest one street off the square; west-bank docks and warehouse front with stepped quays and crane masts.
- **Distinctive Features**: Flood-mark stones on bridge returns; pedestrian refuge bays inset in the bridge; stall-anchor grid set flush with market cobbles; bollard-spaced quay edge with flood ladders and strap-rail spurs.
