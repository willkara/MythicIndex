# Druid Grove Healing Sanctuary – Overview

## Basic Information
- **Type**: Natural Healing Sanctuary
- **Region**: Within Waterdeep’s bounds, sequestered by hedgerows and an oak ring
- **Coordinates/Position**: Concealed woodland hollow reached via discreet city lanes and braided paths
- **First Appearance**: Chapter 19
- **Significance Level**: Important

## Quick Description
A sequestered woodland bowl enclosed by an oak ring and hedgerows, shaped as a living sanctuary: a latticed canopy vault above moss-soft ground, three linked spring-fed crystalline pools, stone-set meditation spirals, and low-impact, braided paths. No built structures—only woven screens, living-wood arches, and set stones integrated into the land’s own restorative systems.

## Visual Summary
- Central Oak Cathedral: veteran oaks with interwoven limbs forming a high green canopy vault
- Three Pools: cleansing → restoration → reflection/scry; reed-edged with pebble aprons and shallow rills between
- Paths & Edges: braided footpaths, stepping setts at damp ground, deadwood hurdles guiding flow and protecting roots
- Meditation Spirals: pebble-and-moss patterns in screened clearings; twig-and-seed chimes at entries
- Herbal Beds: leaf-mold loam, wattle edging; drying racks beneath canopy gaps; labeled stones in druidic script
- Perimeter: holly/hawthorn hedge with discreet openings; bird-rich understory providing sound baffling
