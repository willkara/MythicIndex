## Cultural Atmosphere

### Working-Class Pride
The Merchant's Rest embodies Dock Ward values: practical competence celebrated over aristocratic pretension. When the tradespeople gather here to toast heroes, they focus on technique—how the stone-mason listened to the building, how the water-worker shaped the flow. This is a place where "knowing your trade" is the highest compliment, and where extraordinary deeds are appreciated through the lens of professional skill.
