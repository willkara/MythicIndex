## Social Dynamics

### Values & Discourse [FIRM ch23.5]
- **Core Value**: Professional competence over flash or magic
- **Discussion Focus**: How things were done; technique and method
- **Compliments**: "Knowing your trade" is the highest praise
- **Heroes**: Appreciated for practical skill, not mysterious power

### Storytelling Culture
- **Style**: Eyewitness accounts valued over speculation
- **Details Noted**: Professional technique (stone-listening, water manipulation, military bearing)
- **Tone**: Pride in competence; grounded appreciation
- **Resolution**: Collective acknowledgment through toasts [FIRM ch23.5]

### Crowd Behavior
- **Entry Protocol**: Open and welcoming to working folk
- **Integration**: Patrons share stories freely; conversations blend
- **Celebration Mode**: Standing-room; tankards raised; voices united [FIRM ch23.5]

## Cultural Significance
The Merchant's Rest represents the intersection of labor and legend. Unlike noble salons where heroes are discussed abstractly, here the workers who witnessed events share their accounts. The focus is always on technique: how Grimjaw listened to the stone, how Rill moved water "just so," how Veyra carried herself. Competence is the currency of respect.
