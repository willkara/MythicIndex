## Technology Level
- **Overall Level**: Medieval fantasy with magical augmentation
- **Comparison to Earth Periods**: Late Medieval (14th-15th century) with magic replacing many technological advances
- **Advancement Rate**: Slow - magic fulfills many needs that technology would address

- **Technology Access Inequality**: Significant gap between wealthy/poor, urban/rural, magical/non-magical

- **Local Transportation**: Walking, horses, carts, carriages, magical mounts
- **Long-Distance Travel**: Caravans, ships, teleportation circles (expensive), magical travel

- **Water Transportation**: Sailing ships, barges, magical vessels
- **Speed Capabilities**: Horse (30-40 miles/day), Ship (100+ miles/day with good wind), Teleportation (instant)
