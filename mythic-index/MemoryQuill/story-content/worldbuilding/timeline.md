## Story Timeline

### **The Canyon Incident (Day 0)**

- **The Dragon Fire Ritual**:
  - _Event_: Canyon mission turns catastrophic - dragon fire ritual goes wrong, trapping party members
  - _Characters Involved_: Veyra Thornwake, Lysander Valerius, Borin Stonehand, Elara Whisperwind, Ignis Emberheart
  - _Location_: Undershade Canyon
  - _Chapter/Scene Reference_: Chapter 1 - "Ash and Compass"
  - _Consequences_: Lysander's death, Veyra's scarring, company formation catalyst

### **Recovery Period (Days 1-7)**

- **Day 3 - The Choice**:
  - _Event_: Veyra chooses to keep scars as memorial, begins forming company vision
  - _Characters Involved_: Veyra Thornwake, Dokra Ironvein, Thorne Brightward
  - _Location_: Brightstone Healers' Hall
  - _Chapter/Scene Reference_: Chapter 2 - "The Lantern Ward"
- **Days 4-7 - Legend Building**:
  - _Event_: City reaction spreads, political attempts to control narrative, company formation discussions
  - _Characters Involved_: Multiple city perspectives, Veyra, Thorne
  - _Location_: Various Waterdeep locations
  - _Chapter/Scene Reference_: Chapter 3 - "Echoes in the City"

### **First Active Period (Weeks 1-3)**

- **Week 1-2 - First Mission**:
  - _Event_: Dock Ward fire rescue, first demonstration of competence under Veyra's leadership
  - _Characters Involved_: Veyra, Thorne, Captain Grimsby, Sergeant Kelen, Archer Venn
  - _Location_: Dock Ward, Westwall Watchpost
  - _Chapter/Scene Reference_: Chapter 4 - "The First Spark"
- **Week 2 - Media Response**:
  - _Event_: Newspaper coverage, family gratitude, recruitment planning begins
  - _Characters Involved_: Veyra, Thorne, Captain Grimsby, Dokra, Sara (rescued family)
  - _Location_: Various Waterdeep locations
  - _Chapter/Scene Reference_: Chapter 5 - "Ripples and Reports"

### **Team Building Phase (Weeks 3-6)**

- **Week 3 - First Recruitment**:
  - _Event_: Aldwin Gentleheart recruitment mission at plague village
  - _Characters Involved_: Veyra, Thorne, Aldwin Gentleheart, Dokra, Halden, Mara
  - _Location_: Oakhaven plague village, Brightstone Healers' Hall
  - _Chapter/Scene Reference_: Chapter 6 - "The Healer's Calling"
- **Weeks 4-5 - First Official Contract**:
  - _Event_: Blackvein Lode mine collapse, Grimjaw Ironbeard recruitment
  - _Characters Involved_: Veyra, Thorne, Grimjaw Ironbeard, Aldwin, Darric, Fayne
  - _Location_: Blackvein Lode, Sword Mountains
  - _Chapter/Scene Reference_: Chapter 7 - "The Weight of the World"
- **Week 5 - Team Bonding**:
  - _Event_: Post-mission bonding, gear collection, future planning
  - _Characters Involved_: Veyra, Thorne, Grimjaw, Aldwin, Darric
  - _Location_: Grimjaw's cabin, mountain campsite
  - _Chapter/Scene Reference_: Chapter 8 - "Foundations of Stone and Spirit"

### **Company Formation (Weeks 6-8)**

- **Week 6 - Official Formation**:
  - _Event_: Return from Blackvein, company naming ceremony, Last Light Company founding
  - _Characters Involved_: Veyra, Thorne, Grimjaw, Aldwin, Finn, Mistress Elba, Sarge
  - _Location_: Westwall Watchpost
  - _Chapter/Scene Reference_: Chapter 9 - "The Westwall Welcome"

### **Winter Operations (Months 2-4)**

- **Early Winter - Winter Recruitment**:
  - _Event_: Vera Moonwhisper recruitment, Silent Hand introduction, trafficking discovery
  - _Characters Involved_: Veyra, Vera Moonwhisper, Thorne, Grimjaw, Aldwin, Echo (raven), Thomas & Harriet Blackwood
  - _Location_: Millbridge, Thornwood Forest
  - _Chapter/Scene Reference_: Chapter 10 - "The Tracker in the Snow"
- **Mid-Winter - Growing Pains**:
  - _Event_: Daily routines established, space limitations recognized, community bonding
  - _Characters Involved_: Full company roster, Mistress Elba, Halden, Fayne
  - _Location_: Westwall Watchpost
  - _Chapter/Scene Reference_: Chapter 11 - "Hearth and Home"

### **Expansion Phase (Months 4-6)**

- **Late Winter/Early Spring - Marcus Introduction**:
  - _Event_: Supply run, bandit attack rescue, Marcus Heartbridge introduction
  - _Characters Involved_: Veyra, Thorne, Marcus Heartbridge, Grimjaw, Fayne, Durja, Krag, Lyra, Thom
  - _Location_: Trade Way, The Wayward Compass
  - _Chapter/Scene Reference_: Chapter 12 - "The Wayward Compass" + Chapter 12.5 - "Inn Talk"
- **Spring - First Contract with Marcus**:
  - _Event_: Stonebridge mission, Silas Croft investigation, evidence delivery, Bastion Lock acquisition
  - _Characters Involved_: Veyra, Thorne, Marcus, Grimjaw, Fayne, Silas Croft, Magistrate Vance
  - _Location_: Stonebridge, Azure Flagon, Gilded Gryphon
  - _Chapter/Scene Reference_: Chapter 13 - "The Price of a Key"

### **Current Status**

- **Ongoing - Daily Operations**:

  - _Event_: Established routines, camaraderie development, future planning
  - _Characters Involved_: Full expanded company
  - _Location_: Westwall Watchpost
  - _Chapter/Scene Reference_: Chapter 14.5 - "Watchpost Whispers"

- **Month 4, Day 4-5 - The Long Road Home**:

  - _Event_: Evacuation from Westwall Watchpost, journey to Waterdeep
  - _Characters Involved_: Full Last Light Company, Commander Atlock, Cavalry escort
  - _Location_: Westwall Watchpost to Waterdeep via The Wayward Compass
  - _Chapter/Scene Reference_: Chapter 18 - "The Long Road Home"

- **Month 4, Day 6+ - The Gilded Cage**:

  - _Event_: Arrival in Waterdeep, Lords' Committee hearing, Provisional Civic Asset status
  - _Characters Involved_: Company, Commander Atlock, Lady Aridith, Captain Grimsby, Marcus Heartbridge
  - _Location_: Waterdeep, Dock Ward Warehouse Complex, Castle Waterdeep
  - _Chapter/Scene Reference_: Chapter 19 - "The Gilded Cage"

- **Month 4, Day 6+ - Vera's Healing**:
  - _Event_: Vera transferred to druid grove for specialized healing, introduction of Rill Vossari
  - _Characters Involved_: Veyra, Aldwin, Vera, Rill Vossari, Circle of Druids
  - _Location_: Hidden druid grove in Waterdeep
