# Narrative Patterns

## Writing Style Preferences
- **Sentence Length**: Varied, with shorter sentences during action and stress; longer during reflection
- **Paragraph Structure**: Mixed length, single-line dialogue for impact, longer blocks for description
- **Descriptive Density**: Rich sensory details balanced with direct action
- **Dialogue Style**: Character-specific voices; terse for trauma, gentle for healers, gruff for veterans
- **Exposition Style**: Integrated through action and dialogue rather than info-dumps
- **Action Scene Pacing**: Rapid, visceral, focusing on sensory impact and immediate consequences
- **Emotional Scene Handling**: Raw honesty, allowing vulnerability, avoiding melodrama

## Recurring Themes and Motifs
1. **The Eternal Lantern**:
   - Usage Pattern: Appears as both literal object and metaphor throughout
   - Symbolic Meaning: Hope persisting through darkness, guidance for the lost
   - Associated Characters/Elements: Veyra's survival, Company symbol, "last light" philosophy

2. **Scars as Memory**:
   - Usage Pattern: Physical scars refuse magical healing, emotional scars shape decisions
   - Symbolic Meaning: The importance of remembrance, transformation through trauma
   - Associated Characters/Elements: Veyra's burns, each member's past wounds

3. **"No One Left Behind"**:
   - Usage Pattern: Repeated as vow, philosophy, and action driver
   - Symbolic Meaning: Redemption for past failures, found family bonds
   - Associated Characters/Elements: Company charter, every rescue mission

## Dialogue Style Guide
- **Dialogue Tags Preference**: Minimal tags, action beats preferred for speaker identification
- **Dialect/Accent Handling**: Subtle word choice and rhythm rather than phonetic spelling
- **Internal Thought Formatting**: Italics for direct thoughts, integrated naturally into narrative
- **Interruption Formatting**: Em-dash for cut-offs, ellipsis for trailing off
- **Group Conversation Handling**: Clear speaker attribution, overlapping dialogue shows chaos

## Character Voice Patterns (from Dialogue Expansion)
- **Veyra**: Terse, practical, swallows pain; softer only with deep trust
- **Thorne**: Military precision, protective, formal with hints of warmth
- **Aldwin**: Gentle questions, philosophical comfort, tea ceremony rhythm
- **Grimjaw**: Gruff affection, practical wisdom, protective patriarch
- **Vera**: Observant, cautious trust, nature metaphors in urban settings
- **Marcus**: Smooth diplomacy, calculated charm, moral flexibility
- **Lyra**: Calculating precision, dark humor, cynical wisdom
