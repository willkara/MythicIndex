### Chapter 35 – The Azure Cage <!-- slug: ch35-the-azure-cage -->

**Title**: The Azure Cage  
**Summary**: At a glittering Spring Innovation Gala hosted by Veridian Agronomics, the Last Light Company attends under the guise of professional associates to quietly probe the facility's operations. The team splits cover roles—Finn in the scullery, Cid and Korrath as honored guests, Marcus working the social currents, Rill watching the waters, and Veyra/Thorne holding security posture. Small discoveries accumulate into a troubling picture: an oily pollutant in the harbor, exhausted apprentices, oddly reinforced service areas, and stress fractures suggesting repeated heavy loads beneath the pavilion. Cid deliberately provokes Dr. Mourngrave with a pointed technical remark, forcing a guarded response and securing a formal offer of a facility tour — a polite challenge that signals the Company will be lured into Veridian's inner works. The gala ends with the team aware they have been seen; the planned tour becomes a trap they must now walk into with eyes wide open.  
**Key Events**:
- Infiltration cover set at Azure Pavilion: Finn in scullery, others as guests
- Rill discovers and contains an oily pollutant at the harbor piles
- Grimjaw and Korrath notice structural signs of reinforced cell-like loads
- Cid's public provocation forces Mourngrave to arrange a formal facility tour
- Company leaves with actionable suspicion; tour becomes an acknowledged trap
